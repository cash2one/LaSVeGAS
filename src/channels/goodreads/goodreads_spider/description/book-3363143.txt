3363143:::::Guard Your Tongue: A Practical Guide to the Laws of Loshon Hora:::::Guard Your Tongue is an English adaptation of Rabbi Yisroel Meir Kagan's classic work Chofetz Chayim, a compilation of the laws pertaining to speech. In clear, straightforward language, it is eminently readable. Rabbi Pliskin has added scores of true to life examples to illustrate the practical applications of these laws. This guide is a must for every Jewish home and should be reread frequently.
