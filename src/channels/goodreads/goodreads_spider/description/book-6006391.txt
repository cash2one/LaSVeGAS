6006391:::::No Price Too High: a Novel:::::How does a woman choose between honor and love? Of all the girls at Miss Catchpole's Finishing School, sapphire-eyed Isabella Frost was the fairest. And the wealthiest - until an earthquake half a world away destroyed her family and turned her dreams to dust. In those desperate hours when a marriage of convenience became her only escape, she little dreamed what the future held. From a remote Chilean village to England's lush countryside, her life would be tempered by tragedy, transformed by joy, and dominated by three men: her powerful father, her unforgiving husband, and the brillant young doctor who asked her to believe that, when it come to love, there was no price too high ...
