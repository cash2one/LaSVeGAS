23296722:::::Obsession:::::The twelfth book in the Forever Series sees Aefre obsessed with finding a way to reach her daughter and how that affects her various relationships around her. Her unwitting absence sends her men spiraling towards the dark side, and she has a few choices to make that she knows will change everything. When she is the target of an evil curse that brings with it utter heartbreak for her, she realizes that she has to reevaluate her plans for the future. But when she finally gains access to the one thing she wants more than anything, yet another sickening revelation is unearthed and this time it has consequences for everyone that none of them were prepared for.
