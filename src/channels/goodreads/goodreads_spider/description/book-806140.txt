806140:::::Film as Art:::::In the fall of 1957 the University of California Press expanded Arnheim s 1933 book Film by four essays and brought that landmark work back into print as Film as Art. Now nearly fifty years after that re-edition, the book continues to occupy an important place in the literature of film. Arnheim s method, provocative in this age of technological wizardry, was to focus on the way art in film was derived from that medium s early limitations: no sound, no color, no three-dimensional depth.
