15818041:::::Homestead:::::Home is where the pain is . Kathy Erinson just wanted a hobby, something to help her relax and keep her from getting too bored. What she didn't expect was that the illustrations she started working on would try to tell her secrets best ignored. What she didn't expect was the sudden wash of memories about people and things she'd done her very best to forget about: Her first kiss, her best friend, her family and the dark, dark things that were done once to protect family and homestead alike. Something from the past has come back and that something is very unforgiving.
