6611199:::::A People's History of the United States:::::A classic since its original landmark publication in 1980, Howard Zinn s A People s History of the United States is the first scholarly work to tell America s story from the bottom up - from the point of view of, and in the words of, America s women, factory workers, African Americans, Native Americans, working poor, and immigrant laborers. From Columbus to the Revolution to slavery and the Civil War - from World War II to the election of George W. Bush and the "War on Terror" - A People s History of the United States is an important and necessary contribution to a complete and balanced understanding of American history.
