These new translations unlock the treasures of the Classical texts and will make a valued gift for friends, relatives or business associates.The epic Viking poem, newly translated, tells the beginnings of the world and forecasts its ultimate fate. It is said to have been recorded between 700 and 1000 A.D. and recounts the time when nothing existed, how the gods and men came into being, their initially innocent and joyful life, their conflicts, the destruction of the world, and finally, the world's rebirth.
