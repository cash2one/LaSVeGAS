25590943:::::The River Girl's Song:::::Zillia Bright never dreamed she d be orphaned at sixteen and left to care for her baby brother and Papa s farm. With only a mule and a hundred-year-old shotgun, she must fight to protect what s hers. Countless dangers lurk on the Bastrop Texas riverside. Zillia must rely on the help of her best friends, Soonie and Wylder, to hold her world together. With Zillia s struggles come unexpected miracles, and proof that God might just listen to the prayers of a river girl.
