18163019:::::Ariel's Demise:::::A short twisted tale of our favorite little mermaid; Ariel. In this edition of my Twisted Fairy Tale Collections known also as the "Dark Faerie Tales" series, Ariel wants to become a human. Ursula grants her this wish but for a cost. She has to kill Prince Eric - her love and soulmate, or kill herself and save Eric.... What will you choose?
