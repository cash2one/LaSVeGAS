15772095:::::Naked Want:::::"The Bewitching Blood Series" Book 4, "Naked Want" A vindictive, obsessed and ruthless hunter is after Lucan. The hunter is a witch, Vanessa, and she wants Lucan for herself. As he escapes her grasp, he encounters Miranda but there is something about her which makes his body come alive. In the long life Lucan has had, he has never met a woman as beautiful and sexy as Miranda. The running has made him weary but with the hope Miranda will be in his life, he soon sees a reason to fight. However, Miranda holds a deep dark secret that turns his world upside down. Will their love survive this sexual frenzy and triangle of naked want and passion? It seems Lucan and Miranda have to do the unthinkable to survive Vanessa s terrible grasp.
