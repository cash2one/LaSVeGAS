36241716:::::The Malefactors:::::A Story of The Thieves At The Cross The Malefactors is an epic tale spanning the lifetime of it's characters. Set in Biblical Palestine during the time of Christ, the story follows the life and times of two thieves. With ancient Jerusalem as its background, the inhabitants of the story move from the brutal life of a highwayman to the warmth and security of family and to the ultimate betrayal and a shattering revelation.
