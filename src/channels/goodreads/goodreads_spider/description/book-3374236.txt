3374236:::::Eurotrashed:::::Football hooliganism so long regarded as the 'English Disease', is rife throughout the European game. Yet, while the English scene has been well documented, no one has ever exposed the extent to which the hooligan problem has come to infect the game on the Continent. Until now. Bestselling author and world-renowned hooliganism expert Dougie Brimson has spoken to experts, journalists and even hooligans themselves to examine the truth behind the spread of football violence across Europe. In EUROTRASHED he paints a disturbing picture of just how deeply entrenched in the fabric of European football the culture of hooliganism has become.
