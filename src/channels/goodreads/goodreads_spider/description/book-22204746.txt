22204746:::::An Elephant Is On My House: And Other Poems:::::This is a collection of offbeat and cheerful poems written and illustrated by O. D. D. Cummings. This book is a treasure trove of lyrical and whimsical poetry including: Oh Merry Mocking Bird, Three Tigers And A Hot Air Balloon and of course the humorous, "An Elephant Is On My House". Kids will love the fifteen adorable poems included in this collection!
