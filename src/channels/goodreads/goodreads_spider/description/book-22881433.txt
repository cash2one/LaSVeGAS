22881433:::::The Dark Elf:::::Luke and his team continue to master their powers and skills. After receiving an unknown, life changing inheritance, Luke decides to expand the size of the Silent Soldiers force. Their newest enemy, the Dark Elf, promises to be their greatest challenge so far. His powers are so much more difficult to deal with than anything they have ever encountered. His evil deeds are worse than they imagined to be possible. Follow the Silent Soldiers as they discover and do battle with new enemies, increase the size of their evil fighting force, and deal with all the changes in their lives.
