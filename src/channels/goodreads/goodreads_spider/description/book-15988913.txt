15988913:::::Reaping Me Softly:::::Ever since a near-death-experience on the operating table, seventeen-year-old Arianne Wilson can see dead people. Just as she s learned to accept her new-found talents, she discovers that the boy she s had a crush on since freshman year, Niko Clark, is a Reaper. At last they have something in common, but that doesn t mean life is getting any easier. All while facing merciless bullying from the most powerful girl in school, Arianne s world is turned upside down after Niko accidentally reaps the soul of someone she loves. This sends them both into a spiral that threatens to end Arianne s life. But will Niko break his own Reaper s code to save her? And what would the consequences be if he did?
