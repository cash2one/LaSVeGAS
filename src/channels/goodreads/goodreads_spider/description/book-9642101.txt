9642101:::::Forsaken:::::Ice Moon was only the beginning of Haden and Taylors whirlwind journey. The much anticipated sequal Forsaken, continues the dramatic story of Haden and Taylor as they pick up the pieces of their once shattered foundation and begin to build a new life full of hopes and dreams. As Haden's realities once again take a severe turn down a dark road she must face the morbid truth that her once perfect future has turned into a nightmare of blood and ashes. Suddenly alone in a world of fear and darkness Haden must force herself to embrace the truth. Even the chosen one can be Forsaken.
