305037:::::The Best Supernatural Tales of Algernon Blackwood:::::There is an extraordinary unity in the work of this great author. All his books deal with the strange, supernatural, terror, macabre, other-worldly. He is at his best in his shorter tales &amp; this selection of them was compiled by the author himself at his best. Stories include: The Man who the Trees Loved The Sea Fit The Glamour of the Snow The Tryst Transition The Occupant of the Room The Wings of Horus By Water Malahide and Forden Alexander Alexander The Man who was Milligan The Little Beggar The Pikestaffe Case Accessory before the Fact The Deferred Appointment Ancient Lights You May Telephone from Here The Goblin's Collection Running Wolf The Valley of the Beasts The Decoy Confession A Descent into Egypt
