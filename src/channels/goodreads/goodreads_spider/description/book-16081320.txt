16081320:::::Rock and Roll Never Forgets:::::Beth Morgan is a young woman who s just been given the once in a lifetime opportunity to meet her favorite band, Traveler. That meeting leads to a chance encounter with her idol, the band s lead singer, Andy Stevens. What follows is a love story. A journey through the peaks, valleys and trials of a relationship lived in the public eye; the miles they traveled together through his career in the music business, and her struggles to stay obscure in a world she never really settled into comfortably. In the end it's all about love, fate, and what the heart can't hold back.
