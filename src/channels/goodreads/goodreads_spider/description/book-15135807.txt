15135807:::::Las Aventuras de los Detectives del Faro:::::The Mel family decides to spend their vacation en Norderoog, an island in the North Sea. The island is beautiful, but there is little to entertain the three young Mel sons. To pass time, the three boys decide to help the local Police. They begin to investigate several different crimes: The sale of illegal tobacco, a robbery involving silverware and valuable antiques, and an attempted jewelry robbery. Throughout each story, clues to the crimes appear in footnotes. Readers are able to solve the cases along with the Lighthouse Detectives. Amusing and clever, this selection helps students to develop their deductive reasoning skills and powers of observation.
