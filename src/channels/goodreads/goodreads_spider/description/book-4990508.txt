4990508:::::The Life and Exploits of the Scarlet Pimpernel:::::by John Blakeney (likely pseudonym of John Orczy Barstow), with foreword by Baroness Orczy. This book, published as a biography of Baroness Orczy's popular fictional hero, gives details of the life and family history of Sir Percy Blakeney, Baronet (the Scarlet Pimpernel) as well as members of his intrepid band of adventurers, the League of the Scarlet Pimpernel.
