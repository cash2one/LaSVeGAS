36193:::::Warrior:::::He's a rancher by trade and a warrior by nature -- an ex-CIA Agent who's seen too much of human tragedy not to have it affect his own outlook on life. But then Nevada Blackthorn meets beautiful wildlife biologist Eden Summers and suddenly the world as he knows it is turned upside down. Forced to face his own deeply hidden fears, he must prepare himself to wage war against the greatest challenge ever -- a battle of the heart.
