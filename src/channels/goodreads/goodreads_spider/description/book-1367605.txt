1367605:::::The Dragon at Noonday:::::Wales is gloriously united, while England is torn in two by bloody strife. Llewelyn seizes the opportunity to force recognition of his title and right as Prince of Wales, refusing to be deflected from his goal, even by the tragic deaths of his mother and sister or by his brothers' treachery. But in allying himself with the mighty Earl of Leicester, he stands to win everything...or lose all.
