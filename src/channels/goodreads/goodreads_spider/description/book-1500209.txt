1500209:::::Nobody's Boy:::::A compelling story in which orphaned Remi gets hired out to a traveling street entertainer when his foster parents fall on hard times. Going from village to village with its act, nobody s boy has numerous adventures until his boss also falls on hard times and perishes, homeless and destitute. Remi s life includes a number of surprising twists and turns, leading to a climax and a very happy conclusion when he is reunited with his family.
