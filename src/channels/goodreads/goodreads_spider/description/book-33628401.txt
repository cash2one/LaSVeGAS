33628401:::::Adventures of Huckleberry Finn:::::Adventures of Huckleberry Finn, a novel by Mark Twain, was first published in the United Kingdom in December 1884 and in the United States in February 1885. The book is noted for its colorful description of people and places along the Mississippi River. Set in a Southern antebellum society that had ceased to exist about 20 years before the work was published, Adventures of Huckleberry Finn is an often scathing satire on entrenched attitudes, particularly racism.
