5967064:::::The Photographer:::::In 1986, Afghanistan was torn apart by a war with the Soviet Union. This graphic novel/photo-journal is a record of one reporter s arduous and dangerous journey through Afghanistan, accompanying the Doctors Without Borders. Didier Lefevre s photography, paired with the art of Emmanuel Guibert, tells the powerful story of a mission undertaken by men and women dedicated to mending the wounds of war.
