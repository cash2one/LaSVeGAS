27226128:::::Midnight Hour Collection:::::A couple's road trip for a class reunion takes a bizarre turn into the macabre. A young college student is terrorized by sleep paralysis-stirred by a demonic entity. A hotel clerk is unnerved by a chilling discovery on the second floor. A contractor passes from our world and into the next, witnessing the hellish nightmare that seems to await us all. From the horrifying mind of Russell Simpson, these stories will bring you into a world full of unimaginable nightmares.
