10571:::::The Once and Future King:::::T.H. White s masterful retelling of the Arthurian legend is an abiding classic. The Once and Future King, contains all five books about the early life of King Arthur: The Sword in the Stone The Witch in the Wood The Ill-Made Knight The Candle in the Wind The Book of Merlyn Exquisite comedy offsets the tradegy of Arthur s personal doom as White brings to life the major British epic of all time with brilliance, grandeur, warmth and charm.
