10865022:::::He Loves Me Not:::::This is the first book in the Lily's Story series. "Stay away from him." When college student Lily Jamison receives the ominous note, she convinces herself it's a mistake. After all, she's barely met the two men it could be referring to. But as she begins to fall for the man with the striking blue eyes, the disturbing messages increase. Refusing to believe warnings from an anonymous sender, and trusting her own heart, Lily presses forward with her relationship, eventually coming to understand the meaning behind the messages as she fears for her life. He Loves Me Not does not contain any profanity or sexual content.
