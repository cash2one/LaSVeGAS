11065348:::::Women, Sex And Astrology:::::Sarah Bartlett has fused astrology, mythology and psychology to create a wonderfully rich yet easy-to-use workbook that is a cut above other titles on 'love signs'. This is a unique guide to how each star sign operates as a sexual being, and what qualities they look for in a partner. The reader will be able to create her own unique erotic profile chart, finding out how the all-important Venus and Eros affect the sexual side of the personality. Sarah is a professional astrologer for Cosmopolitan magazine and a specialist in the art of Tarot.
