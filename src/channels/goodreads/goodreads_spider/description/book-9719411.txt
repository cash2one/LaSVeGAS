9719411:::::Ghetto Gurl Memoirs:::::Ignored by her mother and abused by her father Angela, (aka Angel Dust) learns at a very early age that she has a body that men will do and pay anything to get. This knowledge serves her well when she is kicked out to live on her own at sixteen with a baby. Angel Dust shares her pain, failures, victories, lusts and loves in her ghetto girl memoir.
