31280904:::::The Power of Concentration:::::The Power of Concentration was first published in the USA in 1918. As a pioneering self-help book, it deals with developing the faculty of deep concentration to achieve your personal and professional goals. In The Power of Concentration, Dumont describes how you can achieve self-mastery, how to gain what you want through concentration, how to obtain business results through concentration, and how you can use concentration to overcome bad habits, among others.
