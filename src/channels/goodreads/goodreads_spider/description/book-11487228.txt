11487228:::::Shadows in the Nave: A Guide to the Haunted Churches of England:::::A comprehensive history and guide to England's 75 most haunted churches, chapels, and churchyards The haunted history of England's churches and chapels is brought vividly to life in this comprehensive and beautifully illustrated modern guide. Readers will encounter the compelling world of the unseen linked with a thousand years of worship, including the Tudor phantom of Rycote, the lonely monk of Minsden Chapel, and the black-magic ghosts of Clophill, to name but a few. Combining a wealth of historical and paranormal information with stunning original and atmospheric photographs, this volume is perfect for researchers and armchair ghost hunters alike.
