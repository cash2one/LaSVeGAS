18942966:::::Appointment with the Samurai:::::When the plane of wealthy English industrialist Jack Hudson mysteriously crashes in the South American rainforest, the only survivor is his young son, RJ. After fifteen years of searching, the boy s family receive information of his whereabouts and an agent is sent to Japan to bring him back. But the agent soon becomes entangled in a web of torture, murder and betrayal, and he mysteriously disappears. At the centre of suspicion is the criminal organisation of the evil and psychotic Mr A. Major Sandringham is tasked with uncovering the fate of the missing agent. Can Sandringham track RJ down? Can he return the young heir to his family to claim his fortune?
