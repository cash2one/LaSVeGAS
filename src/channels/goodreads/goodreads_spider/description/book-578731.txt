578731:::::I Am the Wallpaper:::::Thirteen-year-old Floey Packer feels like she's always blended in to the background. After all, she's the frumpy younger sister of the Fabulous Lillian. But when Lillian suddenly gets married and heads off on a monthlong honeymoon, Floey decides it's her time to shine. Funny, honest, and surprisingly moving, I Am the Wallpaper introduces a remarkable new talent.
