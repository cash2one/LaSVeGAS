1741794:::::Meggie's Journal:::::Based on the New York Times bestseller by Cornelia Funke When Meggie Folchart discovers that her father has the power to read people and things out of books, she can't believe it. Then she meets some of the characters from the world of INKHEART: Dustfinger who just wants to go home and the evil Capricorn who wants nothing of the kind. Soon Meggie and her father are in a race against time to find a copy of INKHEART, rescue Meggie's mother, and send everyone back where they belong. Peek inside the story with this beautiful full-color, digest-size journal. Told from Meggie s point-of-view, this book will be designed to resemble an actual diary.
