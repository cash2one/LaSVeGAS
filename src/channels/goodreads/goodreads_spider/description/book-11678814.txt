11678814:::::Royal Family:::::Dawn and Elrisa twin princesses of Knurlnien seem to live the flawless, catered life of royalty; parties, suitors and anything they could ever want or need. *Elrisa, does as she is told and lives the life of a pampered princess, perfect with her natural charm and elegance. *Dawn (Never as elegant or charming as Elrisa.) despises her life, and wants nothing more than to fight in the Knurlnien's war against the Deshngler. Determined to do so she runs away to join the war, soon she discovers that nothing is as it seems. And as Dawn begins to uncover the truth behind this conflict, she realizes that her knowledge maybe Knurlnien's only chance at survival.
