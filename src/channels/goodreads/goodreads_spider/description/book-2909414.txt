2909414:::::No Place for Magic:::::Emma and Eadric are finally ready to get married, but Eadric's parents are not so sure about having a witch in the family! The two set out for Upper Montevista, Eadric's homeland to try to make a case for their love, but first Eadric's irritating little brother, Bradston, needs rescuing from some horrible trolls. In a charming twist on the classic fairy tale rescue story, E. D. Baker's hilarious style succeeds in this story tailor-made for her emailing fans who have been demanding "Please can we see the wedding!?"
