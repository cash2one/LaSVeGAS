23830659:::::Pursue Friendship:::::Antisocial cafe worker Frank Friendship is in deep trouble. His work colleague Marek, part-time naked magician, has gone missing and Frank is desperate to hide the events of the night of his disappearance. As if that weren't bad enough, Frank has also acquired a female admirer, Annie, whose goal is to rip off all Frank's clothes. Then there's the severed hand that police have found. The noose is tightening and Frank has never been in greater need of a friend. But is that realisation twenty years too late? This mystery thriller is the second novel in an epic mystery: who is the real Frank Friendship, the murderer's son?
