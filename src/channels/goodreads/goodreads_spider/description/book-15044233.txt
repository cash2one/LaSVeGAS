15044233:::::The Stranger from the Sea:::::It is 1810, and the Poldark family awaits the return of Ross from Portugal. But their ordered existence ends with Jeremy Poldark's dramatic rescue of Stephen Carrington. His arrival changes their lives. For Clowance and Jeremy in particular, the children of Ross and Demelza, Stephen's advent is the key to a new world, one of both love and anger.
