14415420:::::Knighthawke Vanishing Shadows:::::Knighthawke is the code name for USAF special operative Michael Walker. In this second installment of the trilogy Michelle Walker is abducted, taken to Southeast Asia, and held as a bargaining tool to convince Michael to use his enhanced abilities against his own government. During that time, Michelle's family learns that their long, lost son is the special ops agent dispatched to rescue their abducted daughter. Michael must find a way to avoid becoming a traitor, rescue his sister, and return to America so that he can meet the biological family that he has so longed for.
