32867786:::::Embracing Hope:::::Take a poignant look into the broken heart and daily struggles of Christian college dean Drew McKinley. The story chronicles the turbulent first year in his desperate journey to understand God s motives for the sudden death of his wife and his quest to find hope in his future. Crossing his perilous path are Allison, a graduate student and new employee in the dean s office, and Chris, the handsome but egotistical student senate president. The road Drew must navigate is fraught with career upheaval, a reawakening heart and the struggle for forgiveness and restoration. Will Drew finish his journey to embrace the hope God offers, the love Allison shares and the guidance Chris needs, or will he turn his back on all three with catastrophic consequences?
