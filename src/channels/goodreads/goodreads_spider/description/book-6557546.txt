6557546:::::The Curse of Ambrose:::::What if your dreams were consumed with someone you ve never met? In a world that shouldn't exist? Ellie Mancini's life changes drastically when she discovers a hidden door. A door that leads into a dark world of vampires and witches. She faces fears she never knew existed and struggles with the agonizing twists of her discoveries that only continue to grow deeper and darker. She doesn't know who to trust and her only chance of getting back into her own world is with the help of the vampire who was born to protect her.
