18285893:::::The Ring:::::Alexandra is determined to know her true nature. However, with the truth, comes love, intertwined with lust, infatuation and unfulfilled desires. Not only does she need The Ring to stay alive, she also must kill The Vangel. Soon she's tangled in a love triangle if not quadrangle of which one of her close companions is The Vangel. One lifeline.
