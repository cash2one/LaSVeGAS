1639713:::::Troublesome Creek:::::A charming historical novel set in the late 1800s. Born and raised in the hills of Kentucky, Laura Copper Grace loves the wilderness of her home in Troublesome Creek. But when her stepmother threatens to send her away to boarding school to become a lady, Copper faces the possibility of losing everything that is precious to her. Copper must come to terms with her family and discover the true meaning of home. Nothing can drag her off the mountain, until the day she realizes that God has other plans for her life.
