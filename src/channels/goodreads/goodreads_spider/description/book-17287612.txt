17287612:::::Earth Force I:::::YA Science Fiction Jason Lyons is one of the top competitors in the World Championships for Earth Force, a mech fighter video game created by billionaire tech tycoon, Howard Hunt. When aliens show up on Earth with hostile intentions, Hunt recruits the top Earth Force gamers to defend the planet from certain destruction. Written in the style of a Japanese 'light novel' Earth Force I is a fun and action packed Science Fiction adventure.
