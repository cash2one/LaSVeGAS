26849943:::::The Wulver's Rose:::::From the acclaimed anthology, FIVE ENCHANTED ROSES, comes a tale of romance and second chances set in the Highlands of Scotland! A life is a high price to pay for stealing a rose. But Bonnie vowed long ago to never again let her family suffer because of her actions. So when her father returns from his travels with stories of a remote, ruinous castle and a terrible creature demanding restitution for theft, she determines to take his place. Alone Bonnie journeys to the home of this legendary beast known as a wulver half man, half wolf. Can she survive imprisonment by such a monster? Is there more to this wulver than meets the eye? And is he somehow connected to the frightened child who visits Bonnie in her dreams?
