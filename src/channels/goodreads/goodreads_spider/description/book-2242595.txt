2242595:::::Scorpion, a Good Bad Horse:::::A fine-looking chestnut bronc receives the name Scorpion when he explodes unexpectedly after several days of unusually good behavior. "That horse, " a cowboy says, "is going to be either mighty good or mighty bad." Tim, an outlaw on the run, trades his tired horse for Scorpion. The horse acts truly wicked only when Tim makes poor decisions--keeping him out of trouble.
