12844448:::::Insectland:::::High school student Dan Larson thought he swatted a bug. Turns out it is a reconnaissance soldier from a miniature alien attack force planning to invade Earth. Kidnapped to another planet Dan is the shrunk to the size of an ant, enslaved, and forced into a labor camp with thousands of other captured alien species. There is hope. Legions of insects, some alive, some robotic, have brought Dan into their private lair. Intent on stopping the invasion they have a risky plan which includes his involvement. Dan puts his life on the line risking everything in a desperate attempt to prevent the enslavement of all free societies. But is it too late?
