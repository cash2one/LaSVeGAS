521147:::::Cade:::::A tale of addiction and obsession, and the dangers of both. Cade was at the top of his game. He was a photographer wanted by everyone. And then, on a beach in Acapulco, he meets Juana Roca. Despite warnings that she is a danger to men, he proceeds to fall in love. From here things begin to spiral downwards as she saps the will out of him, drink begins to take hold and suddenly the money and the work are no longer there.
