200335:::::The Lady or the Tiger and Other Stories:::::1887. Thonias Hewlings Stockton, clergyman, wrote fantastic tales for children under the pen name Frank R. Stockton. He also wrote books for adults. Among his amusing books for children Rudder Grange is the best known. The Lady and the Tiger is one of his most popular works. Contents: The Lady, or the Tiger?; The Transferred Ghost; The Spectral Mortgage; Our Archery Club; That Same Old 'Coon; His Wife's Deceased Sister; Our Story; Mr. Tolman; On the Training of Parents; Our Fire-Screen; A Piece of Red Calico; and Every Man His Own Letter-Writer.
