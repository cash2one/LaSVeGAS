17905237:::::Not That Kind of Love:::::In Not That Kind of Love, Michelle is a savvy young modern woman who has her life and her career together. She is in love with her best friend that she has known since kindergarten. Michelle wants more but is too shy to pursue him and only admires him from afar. He usually pursues another type of woman and she does not fit the bill. When he decides that it is Michelle that he wants to settle down with, she is suddenly afraid that she could lose everything if it doesn t work out.
