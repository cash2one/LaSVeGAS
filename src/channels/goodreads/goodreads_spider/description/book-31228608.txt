31228608:::::A Selfless Man:::::What would you do to save your son? How far would you go? Roland Moses was a college running back, the greatest of all time and first round NFL draft pick. Within days of his dream he commits a vicious crime and his world is destroyed. Years later, the hell of prison life over he returns to the outside only to find a new hell. A hell no father should have to endure. Author Shawn Raymond Poalillo is the author of Rerun: A New Face of Terrorism and a retired Special Agent from the Department of Homeland Security with over twenty-four years of federal law enforcement experience.
