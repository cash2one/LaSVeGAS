17259417:::::Dragon's Fury:::::In book 2, Dragon's Fury, two 10-year-old boys, Zeth and Chase must go on a quest to save Zeth's mother who has been cursed. They must battle evil Lord V who has taken over the Dragon King's Cave Kingdom and is planning on destroying the king himself and all his heirs. Zeth knows that he has to defeat Death and is only given 2 weeks to find the cure, save his mother, and fight the dragon.
