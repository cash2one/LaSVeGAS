36629470:::::CAPTIVE:::::A captivating tale of love, power and betrayal. When Katherine, an unworldly, innocent maid, is sold as a slave to the prince of Wallachia, she is terrified of the man who is whispered to be a sorcerer, a monster or even the devil himself. Imprisoned in his castle, Katherine is shocked by the realization that she is drawn to this enigmatic man and despite his terrifying nature, finds she is desperate to get close to him. The prince, intrigued by Katherine's beauty, courage and independent spirit, finds himself in an uncomfortable predicament - falling in love with the women he must kill to save his kingdom.
