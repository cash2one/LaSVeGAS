30069169:::::Requiem for Immortals:::::Professional cellist Natalya Tsvetnenko moves seamlessly among the elite where she fills the souls of symphony patrons with beauty even as she takes the lives of the corrupt of Australia s ruthless underworld. The cold, exacting assassin is hired to kill a woman who seems so innocent that Natalya can t understand why anyone would want her dead. As she gets to know her target, she can t work out why she even cares.
