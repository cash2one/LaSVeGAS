293459:::::Akiko: The Menace of Alia Rellapor, Volume 2:::::This second installment of the first AKIKO trilogy collects issues 8 through 13 of the on-going AKIKO comic book series. In this book, Akiko and her friends continue their journey to rescue the Prince of Smoo from the clutches of Alia Rellapor. Along the way they encounter the insurmountable Great Wall of Trudd, are reunited with P.Q. Goybi, face off with the villainous Loza Throck, and finally reach Alia Rellapor's gargantuan palace! Written and illustrated by Mark Crilley.
