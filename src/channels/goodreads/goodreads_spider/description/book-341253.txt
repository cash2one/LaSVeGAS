341253:::::Pargeters:::::In a largely Puritan section of England, strong-willed Sarah Woodley's Royalist leanings trapped her in a dangerous web of political intrigue. And when she saved a handsome young man with a Puritan price on his head, she knew if they were caught, both would die. But neither war nor tyranny would take away the man she loved--or the house she so proudly possessed!
