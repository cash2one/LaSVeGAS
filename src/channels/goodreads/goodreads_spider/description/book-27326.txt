27326:::::The Wall:::::Riveting &amp; compelling, The Wall tells the inspiring story of forty men &amp; women who escape the dehumanizing horror of the Warsaw ghetto. John Hersey's novel documents the Warsaw ghetto both as an emblem of Nazi persecution &amp; as a personal confrontation with torture, starvation, humiliation &amp; cruelty--a gripping, visceral story, impossible to put down.
