12109683:::::A Fair to Remember:::::Reformed gang member Wes Carter feels Redemption, Wisconsin, is just the place to relocate his financial business for reasons more than just the name. He s ready for a nice, quiet life in small town USA. Tara Russell has decided it s time to add some excitement to her life and get a little wild no matter how uncomfortable it makes her or her overprotective family. They meet at the local fair one warm summer night and discover opposites attract for all the right reasons. Add a dash of Sugar in the form of an incorrigible Great Dane, and it s destined to be A Fair To Remember.
