2290538:::::Guilt by Silence:::::CIA analyst Mariah Bolt was devastated when a vicious attack left her brilliant husband brain-damaged -- but finding out she was the intended victim and that the CIA had known all along added betrayal and terror to her grief. Now she has to expose a plot to smuggle nuclear weapons to ruthless terrorists, a plot involving all those she thought she could trust the most -- from her employers to her husband himself....
