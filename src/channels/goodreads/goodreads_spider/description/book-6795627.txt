6795627:::::Second Chances:::::When Brianna Amoretti catches her husband of a little over a year in bed with his secretary, her life is turned upside-down. She decides to make a fresh start, hoping to figure out who she is and where she is heading. Spending a month alone at a friend's beach house in Stone Harbor, New Jersey is part of the big plan. However, her plans take an unexpected twist when a handsome stranger's dog knocks her down on the beach. William Kent is all that she thinks she wants in a new relationship, but he seems to be hiding something from her and she cannot quite put her finger on what. Second Chances is the story of two lost souls coming together to find an unexpected love, if only they can get over their biggest obstacles: each other!
