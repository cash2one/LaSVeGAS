1262418:::::Lady of the Haven:::::Beautiful, daring Stacy leaves the safety of the Haven to set out on a dangerous journey across vast seas to the forbidden land of the white wolves. Here she discovers the fabled lost city of Satra, besieged by barbarian hordes who ravage it from above, while things , half human and half baboon, attack it from below. And all around wait savage wolves who speak as humans do and whose queen is a woman.
