17993295:::::Minutes Before Sunset:::::Eric has less than a year left before his fated battle when he meets her. She's a shade like him, but separated from the Dark - and with more power than she should have. And he can't stay away. Jessica is new to town and desperate to figure out who, or what, her birth parents were. But she can't find them without overcoming Eric, and she won't let him stop her. 'Minutes Before Sunset' happens through the minds of two young adults as they struggle to survive their paranormal realities and find balance in their human lives.
