29811:::::Little Pilgrim's Progress: From John Bunyan's Classic:::::Fifty-five years ago, Helen L. Taylor took John Bunyan's Pilgrim's Progress and simplified the vocabulary and concepts for young readers, while keeping the storyline intact. The result was a classic in itself, which has now sold over 600,000 copies. It's both a simple adventure story and a profound allegory of the Christian journey through life, a delightful read with a message kids ages 6 to 12 can understand and remember. A new look and fresh illustrations for today's children enlivens the journey to the Celestial City. Book Details: Format: Paperback Publication Date: 2/1/2006 Pages: 336 Reading Level: Age 4 and Up
