18187149:::::Dance at Midnight:::::Dance at Midnight is a love story based on a young girl named, Emily Grayson, living in a small mining town in Nanty Glo, Kentucky during the 50's. Emily's dream has always been to get out of Nanty Glo and move to a bigger town. When she meets Austin Payne she must face the pains of growing up and falling in love before she can make a choice. But, first she must face the heartache of a mining cave-in that may cause her father, James Grayson's, death. Dance at Midnight is a heart warming story of a daughter's love for her father and the man she wants to be with..
