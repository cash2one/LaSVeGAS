26206590:::::Promise Kept:::::"Deception in the courtroom . Self-declared ice queen attorney Cara Phillips has a secret; a secret that she wanted to be free of. She has no one to turn to except Romaine Jacobs is a fellow attorney, but also the next in line to inherit the kingdom of Jacob s law firm in downtown Chicago; who also has a secret of his own. He is in love with Cara and she has kept the cold between them for years until The ice cold shell began to crack when they were put on the same case; a case that was a reminder to Cara of a violent and dangerous past that she would never forget. Will Cara tell her secret and put their lives in danger? Or will a promise kept save them?"
