171807:::::The Dog in the Manger:::::While the countess secretary is posing as a son of a statesman, the countess is free to marry him. But when he admits his deception, the countess is faced with a social dilemma. A painful and hilarious comedy for anyone who has ever fallen in love with someone they shouldn t have fallen in love with, The Dog in the Manger was part of the Royal Shakespeare Company s acclaimed Spanish Golden Age Season in 2004.
