13474884:::::BrownBeat Photo Companion to Hampi:::::The BrownBeat Photo Companion to Hampi provides almost 90 stunning photos of the abandoned magical city of Hampi in Southern India, as a complementary publication to the BrownBeat Guide to Hampi. This eBook takes you on a visual journey of the ruins, accompanied by a generous amount of descriptions of each site and each photo. Whilst the BrownBeat Guide to Hampi includes practical travel information, this gallery of visual splendor provides you with a virtual tour of Hampi's key sites.
