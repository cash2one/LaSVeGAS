224521:::::The Man with Bad Manners:::::This story is about a badly behaved man and how a young boy initiates a plan to change his behavior and, with the help of all the villagers, succeeds. It will bring laughter to young children and at the same time teach them valuable lessons about conflict resolution, initiative and cooperation. Rose Mary Santiago's illustrations provide delightful and amusing counterpoint to the story. This is the third book she has illustrated in this series.
