823182:::::Why Men Lie And Women Cry:::::Allan and Barbara Pease are the world's foremost experts in personal relationships. Their books, seminars and TV programmes have made them household names from Australia to the UK and from the USA to Japan. In this, their first new book since the multi-million selling WHY MEN DON'T LISTEN AND WOMEN CAN'T READ MAPS, Allan and Barbara use the same combination of startling observation of people's action toward one another, humour and practical advice to teach the reader more about what men want from relationships and what women want from relationships and how to get it.
