239870:::::You Belong to Me and Other True Crime Cases:::::In "You Belong to Me, " Rule fixes her unsparing professional gaze on one of Florida's most shocking criminal cases - that of Tim Harris, a poster-perfect "all-American" Florida State Trooper who hid bizarre and fatal fantasies behind his badge - plus other incredible true crime cases from her personal files. Mesmerizing us with her masterful exploration of the personalities and backgrounds of the criminals and their families, and the circumstances surrounding each crime, Rule again proves that she is "the undisputed master crime writer of the eighties and nineties" (John Saul).
