13721291:::::Love Plus One:::::With her divorce final, Stephanie moves to Utah looking forward to finding the freedom and independence denied to her throughout her marriage. What she didn't expect was meeting the two sexy men next door. Forming an instant friendship with them, they soon take Stephanie on a path of self-exploration wilder than anything she could imagine. When her ex-husband calls making threats, she fears the happiness she's found will once again be taken away. As it becomes clear her ex won't stop until she is back under his control, the three of them work together to try and keep Stephanie safe. With the ghosts of her past haunting her every move, will her new life be over before it even started?
