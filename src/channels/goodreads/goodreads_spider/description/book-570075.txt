570075:::::Riding the Wind:::::Angie dreams of owning Lila, the white Arabian she's been riding for three years at Smitty's Good Luck Ranch. Thanks to an unexpected inheritance, she has a chance to buy her dream horse-but she must move fast. Sage, Angie's rival at the stable, has her eye on Lila, too. Then, one morning, Sage takes Lila on a trail ride-and what happens shatters Angie's world.
