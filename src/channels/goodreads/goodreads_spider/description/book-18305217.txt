18305217:::::Love vs. Lust:::::Six months have passed since Holly Adams parents were murdered and her hometown massacred. All summer long she along with her Guardian Angel boyfriend, and the Prince of Hell have been running... Hunted by both sides Holly has been whisked all over the globe in a frantic bid to keep her from Michael, the Archangel intent on her demise. Faced with an uncertain future all she has to relay on is herself and her two protectors, whom she treasures more than life itself. Holly must find a way to dodge the Devil, Heaven and Michael, all of whom want her for their own ends... After Caleb makes a heart wrenching choice, his decision inadvertedly leads Holly to the biggest confrontation of our time...
