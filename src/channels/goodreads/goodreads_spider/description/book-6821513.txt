6821513:::::Not Suitable for Family Viewing:::::Robin has everything a girl could want. Thanks to her mother the internationally beloved talk show host Mimi Schwartz Robin s got the money, the means and the connections to make even her wildest dreams come true. So why, then, does she choose to sit alone in a dark room watching endless reruns of you, you and Mimi? Don t ask Robin. She doesn t know not, at least, until the bizarre discovery of an old high-school ring propels her to ditch New York for a tiny Nova Scotian fishing village. In her quest to solve the mystery behind the ring s origin, Robin finds more than a renewed joie de vivre. She discovers love, the truth of her own background and the shocking secret that helped make her mother a star.
