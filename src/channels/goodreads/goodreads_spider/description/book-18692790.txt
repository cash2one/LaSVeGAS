18692790:::::Bullet Catch:::::Bullet Catch is the first novel in the Smoke &amp; Dagger series where the worlds of espionage and illusion collide. A Russian killing machine escapes his handlers in Dubai. CIA operatives are targeted in Berlin. A horrifying murder in a quiet suburb. How are they connected? Steve Watson, son of famous television magicians Mike and Nora Watson, accompanies his parents on a trip to Berlin for one last show. But Mike and Nora s secret past of working with the CIA comes back to haunt them, and they vanish into thin air when a hit squad attacks. Now CIA operative Dana Fox and the Berlin Police think Steve s the key to unraveling the killings. Unfortunately for him, he is. And Steve s greatest trick will be to figure out the connection before it s too late...
