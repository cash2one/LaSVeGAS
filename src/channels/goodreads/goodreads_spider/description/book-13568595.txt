13568595:::::Deadly Offerings:::::Anne Mason thinks she ll be safe living in the Midwest building a wind farm. She may be dead wrong. Someone is dumping bodies in her corn field and telling Anne they are gifts for her! As the body count rises, Anne realizes a cold-blooded serial killer is patiently waiting and watching her every move. And he won t stop until he ends her life. It is clear there are no limits to this killer s thirst for revenge or how far he will go to get it. Michael Brandt, the ruggedly handsome new County Prosecutor, is the last person Anne can trust, but may offer her only chance at survival from a psychopathic killer. But will she survive the passion that rages between them?
