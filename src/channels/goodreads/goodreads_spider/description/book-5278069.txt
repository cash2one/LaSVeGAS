His comments on Hockey Night in Canada s "Coach's Corner" routinely make headlines as they entertain, educate, and often upset some fans throughout North America. Written with veteran sports journalist Al Strachan, here are Don Cherry's favourite stories from his career in hockey. And you can imagine the stories he has to tell.
