22725056:::::Antar and Haider the Lion:::::Young readers story of Antar the Black Knight and his adventures. In this episode, the young Antar finds Haider, an injured lion who has been hunting farm animals from his village. King Zoheir, has offered a reward to the one who brings him the lion. While everyone in Antar's village is determined to hunt down the lion and kill him, Antar takes pity on the poor creature and nurses him back to health. Once Haider is strong again, Antar sets him free, gaining a friend for life. Little does young Antar know, one day Haider will return the favor...
