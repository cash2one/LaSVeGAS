19547700:::::Play With Me:::::After the death of his parents Kat is forced to live in a new world full of luxury and power, there she meets Robert Leigh and Nick Swanson the guys with more money and power. one of them offers her love and a life that had never imagined and and the other is determined to ruin her life. She will have to learn about love, friendship and power as she tries to discover herself. There is only one thing to learn, the life of a millionaire is not as pretty as it looks.
