1932023:::::Prayers from a NonBeliever:::::So begins a series of letters addressed to God from an anonymous young artist in this new book from the bestselling author of The Artist's Way . Following in the tradition of Letters to a Young Poet and Conversations with God , this is Julia Cameron's most inspiring book to date. In the form of letters from the heart, Prayers from a Non-Believer probes questions central to the artist's journey-how can we preserve our creative selves in our busy modern lives? What is the meaning of art and how does it relate to spirituality? This book is a unique treasure from one of the great teachers of our time.
