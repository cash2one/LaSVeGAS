16100576:::::The Zombie Cause Dictionary:::::Inspired by The Zombie Cause, the world s first documentary short film series highlighting the must-win battle against zombies, this book will introduce you to Doc Ruger, the world s most notable authority on zombology. As a prestigious zombologist, Doc Ruger instructs his three sons in zombie behavior and the art of zombie hunting. His boys, Headshot, Silent Blade, and ZB (Zombie Boy) our country s teens and tweens are our nation s last hope. Either this generation learns of the threat and becomes skilled in zombie hunting, or our great nation will fall to the Horde.
