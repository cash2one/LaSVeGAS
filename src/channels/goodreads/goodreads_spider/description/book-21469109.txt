21469109:::::Finding Serendipity:::::When Tuesday McGillycuddy and her beloved dog, Baxterr, discover that Tuesday's mother the famous author Serendipity Smith has gone missing, they set out on a magical adventure. In their quest to find Serendipity, they discover the mysterious and unpredictable place that stories come from. Along the way, she learns what it means to be a writer and how difficult it can sometimes be to get all the way to The End.
