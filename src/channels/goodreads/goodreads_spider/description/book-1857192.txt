And from the first moment that Mary Carmichael lifted her violet eyes to those of Hunter MacBeth, the prophecy began to come true. Hunter was Mary's kidnapper - and her lover. He begged a king for her hand, and she braved secret torture to be true. Theirs is a legend of struggle against relentless hate, of two wild hearts who pledged defiantly, Forever my Love
