8299697:::::Akiko Flights of Fancy:::::Collectors of Mark Crilley's AKIKO comic books have long had a well-kept secret all to themsleves: the ongoing procession of fun features, pin-ups, and gags that has marched through the back pages of this series from the very start. AKIKO: FLIGHTS OF FANCY brings together these little-seen works for the first time. These rare pages represent some of Crilley's most creative work to date.
