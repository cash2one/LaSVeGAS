21346745:::::Ahh! Sims.:::::Ahh! Sims. Amusing, often poignant semi-autobiographical tales of a ten year old boy trying to grow up in sixties London. I was a perfectly natural lad - undisciplined, uninterested, uninspired, uncaring and any other 'un' you can think of. I spent my free time being as obnoxious as possible - I created havoc where peace once reigned and constantly advised teachers why they had got the wrong boy.
