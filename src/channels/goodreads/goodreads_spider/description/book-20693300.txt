20693300:::::Thanksgiving Surprises:::::Isaac, Abe and John Troyer prepare to inform their Aunt Rachael that she must move into a dawdy haus, the small Amish cottage for retired parents, when they receive the shock of their lives. John has a long talk with Helen Mathias where Helen does most of the talking. In the end they reach a final decision about their relationship. Jasmine creates more havoc in the small Amish community and threatens to ruin the Troyer s large Thanksgiving celebration. Fortunately Zeke, with the attempted assistance of Abe, puts a stop to that.
