18469521:::::Vacation:::::Leaving small town Idaho behind, Liz accompanies her mother on a trip to California to meet her boyfriend. She looks forward to lazy days on the beach and a lot of sunshine while her mother is distracted with her man. When Mr. Tall, Dark, and Handsome shows up at the beach, Liz is more than willing to spend some of her down time enjoying his company, after all, he's everything a girl could dream of. Leo sweeps her off her feet by treating her like a princess in public and a whore in bed. The chemistry between them is undeniable, but fate has a way of throwing a curve ball into anything good. When Liz finds out who Leo really is, decisions have to be made that just might put a cold freeze on their summer vacation.
