5067665:::::Thank My Lucky Scars:::::Ward Foley, aka Scarman , survived decades of disfigurement, pain, and suffering. But it was through the death of a close friend that he learned a profound lesson and discovered a secret that changed his life forever. This is a story about finding what you want most, in the most unlikely places, and usually right under your nose. Share the joy of seeing your own life and everything around you in bold new ways.
