6338123:::::Cry for the Moon:::::What would you do, if the people you loved wanted you to become a monster? What if they wouldn't take no for an answer? When Zach Trewick is faced with questions like these, there seems to be only one choice he can make. . . Run. Sleeping under bridges and raking leaves to scrounge money, he gives up the only life he's ever known and goes in search of his uncle Justin, the only person he can think of who might take him in. But the journey is a long one, and Zach soon discovers that being true to what he believes in can somtimes carry a higher price than he ever imagined.
