3046821:::::Now This War Has Two Sides:::::Examining the premises and core elements of his controversial and groundbreaking books, this two-hour lecture and discussion spotlights author and environmental activist Derrick Jensen. Providing a perfect introduction for newcomers as well as additional insight for those already familiar with Jensen s work, this argument courageously exposes the ravages of industrial civilization. Relaying humorous anecdotes from the presenter s life and bravely presenting some of the endless forms that resistance can and must take, this discourse leaves its audience both engaged and enraged.
