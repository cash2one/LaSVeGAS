25573718:::::Shadows of the Inferno:::::It was said that the house was a shadow. That it was smoke. It was a thing that existed only as an image cast, showing the absence of light. The stories of the horrors that occurred within the walls of the Weeping House have become legend. However, the truth is far more sinister than any could have imagined and the house in the woods by Howard's Crossing is the place where the ultimate battle between good and evil will be fought in the darkness cast by the Shadows of the Inferno.
