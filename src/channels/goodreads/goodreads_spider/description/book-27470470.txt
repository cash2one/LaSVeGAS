27470470:::::Captured Moments: Inspiration Captured in Verse:::::Life is experienced one moment at a time. Capturing those instants can be quite the challenge.This third poetry collection by JD Estrada is his literary attempt at capturing a thought, an emotion, or a moment at its most raw and alive. Some people use video. Others use photography. Estrada has chosen words as his tool of choice and he hopes you enjoy the day's catch.
