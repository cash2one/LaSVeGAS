13142811:::::The Drive:::::The Drive is a true page turner into the dangerous and unexpected. Marvelously well written, this is an astounding story with an absolutely rich and intriguing plot that doesn t disappoint. Each chapter will keep you pinned to your seat, then leaves you breathless with a heart wrenching surprise ending. Brian Hadley needed to reconnect with his family. In hopes of salvaging what is left of their parting relationships he designed what he thought to be the perfect family get away, a road trip to a remote mountain Lodge. Along the way Brian, his wife Kathy and their three children discover much more than their family bonds with each other as they struggle for survival in a foreboding, deadly and treacherous land.
