6455548:::::NERDS: National Espionage, Rescue, and Defense Society:::::A group of unpopular students are part of a spy network inside their school. With the help of cutting-edge science, their nerdy qualities are enhanced and transformed into incredible abilities. They battle the Hyena, a former junior beauty pageant contestant turned assassin, and an array of James Bond style villains, each with an evil plan more diabolical and more ridiculous than the last.
