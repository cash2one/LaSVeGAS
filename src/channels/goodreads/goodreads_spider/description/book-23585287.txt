23585287:::::Enslavement:::::"One world. One currency. One bright future." That's the promise made by OneEarth Bank after a global economic collapse--but only for those who accept the insertion of a commerce chip. When Rielle's parents refuse to comply, government officials tear her family apart. As punishment for her parent's crimes, Rielle is forced into a Community Service Contract--a legalized form of slavery--and sold to a wealthy, abusive banker. The Banker's secrets hold the key to Rielle's freedom, but will she risk prison or even death to escape and search for her family?
