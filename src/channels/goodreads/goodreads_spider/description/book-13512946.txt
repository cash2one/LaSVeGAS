13512946:::::Little Star:::::A man finds a baby in the woods, left for dead. He brings the baby home, and he and his wife raise the girl in their basement. When a shocking and catastrophic incident occurs, the couple s son Jerry whisks the girl away to Stockholm to start a new life. There, he enters her in a nationwide singing competition. Another young girl who s never fit in sees the performance on TV, and a spark is struck that will ignite the most terrifying duo in modern fiction.
