31327374:::::The Legend of Sleepy Hollow:::::"The Legend of Sleepy Hollow" was first published in 1820. Sleepy Hollow is known for being home to ghosts and spirits, the most famous being the terrifying Headless Horseman. The Legend of Sleepy Hollow is highly entertaining fiction. It has been adapted many times for stage, theatre, television and film, including the 1999 Tim Burton film starring Johnny Depp and Christina Ricci, and the hit television show, Sleepy Hollow, starring Tom Mison, Nicole Beharie, and Orlando Jones.
