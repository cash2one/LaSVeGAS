162920:::::The Devil Knows You're Dead:::::Scudder is back, tracking a killer through the alleys of Hell s Kitchen and mapping the darker regions of the human soul. Glenn Holtzman sits on top of the world, watching the sun set from his penthouse half and hour later he s just another statistic, gunned down in a phone booth on Eleventh Avenue. When the cartridge casings of the fatal shots turn up on a well-known local Vietnam Vet the whole of the Big Apple knows it s an open and shut case. But not Scudder this Yuppie lawyer has skeletons in his closet and Matt can hear them rattling.
