15707938:::::a small lie:::::Katrina, desperate to land her dream job, tells her boss she is married. Her imaginary husband Paul, is based on a man whom she once had passionate one-night affair with six months ago. But when he turns up at her office one day he gets swept along with the lie. Then they are invited by the boss to a four day trip to Scotland, and she has no choice but to take her husband along. Not only must she make sure no one discovers her secret, she must also stop herself from falling in love with a wildly spontaneous man.
