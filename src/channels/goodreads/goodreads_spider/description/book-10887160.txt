10887160:::::Making a Diamond:::::In a bit of a role reversal story, Sandi Layne offers a romance between a man of faith and emotion and a woman with ambition. Preston Adams and Rhiannon Reilly have known each other all their lives, but for the patriarch of Rhiannon's family, it's not enough. He believes they should be married and he's determined to make it happen. To Preston, this is a gift from God Himself. To Rhiannon, it's a confusing distraction from her goals. But she will do anything for her beloved Great-grandfather. Pressured from all sides, Rhiannon will find herself shaped by the transforming power of love.
