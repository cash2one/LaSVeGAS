3882844:::::The Saga of Pederasticles and His Boy:::::Penguin recently reprinted this rare text detailing one of histories greatest love stories. This is the tale of Pederasticles the boy he loves the trials he must endure so that they may be together. For the Penguin Classics edition, noted Greek historian and language expert Colin Wentworth was commissioned to translate the epic saga from its original Greek text.
