1071760:::::A Mother's Gift:::::A stirring and heartfelt novel about the special bond between one mother and daughter, "A Mother's Gift" follows 14-year-old Holly Faye Lovell, a rising young singer. Now that Holly's off with her posh new friends and new life, she's ashamed to find herself embarrassed by her mom and their humble background. Meanwhile, her mother finds herself wanting to reveal a long-hidden secret that could destroy their bond forever.
