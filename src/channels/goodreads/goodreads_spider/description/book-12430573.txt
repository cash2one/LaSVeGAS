12430573:::::Shadows of Blood:::::Shadows of Blood, the second book in the Night Feast trilogy After being killed by the vampire Lia, Elena Hudson is raised from her cremated ashes, back to her form as a human, teenage girl. However, she has been given the power and the task of destroying Lia forever. With the help of Dalia Jackson she plans to kill her enemy while she is in a vulnerable position, but they do not predict that Jay will thwart Elena's attempt to kill his vampire lover.
