23209888:::::Anne Hawksmoor: The Time Thief:::::Anne Hawksmoor: The Time Thief, is BOOK THREE of the Anne Hawksmoor series. It follows the time-travelling adventures of eleven year old Anne Hawksmoor on her visit from Chicago to Greenwich and the Tower of London in England. Anne is a bullied pre-teen who finds herself unable to relate to her peers at school. In Book Three, Anne and her cousin Claire have just found a way back from 1548 to the present. Shortly after Anne and Claire's return, their young cousin John runs away to have his own experience of time-travel. Anne and Claire must now find (or make!) a way back in time to rescue John.
