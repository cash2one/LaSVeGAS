18039077:::::Hell on Ice:::::A young mother, Tori, is on her way home to her house in Denver when a nuclear detonation occurs in the city of Denver. Tori acts quickly and resolves to do whatever it takes to provide shelter for herself and her 3-year-old daughter, Elizabeth until such time that some form of order can be re-instated. Not far from where Tori and Liz settle, two bikers wake up to a brand new mid-day. The mushroom cloud on the horizon tells them that it's going to be a GREAT day. Can Tori and Liz win a battle against nothing more sinister than the winter weather? Can anyone survive an encounter with Carl and Trey? (Note: This book should be read by adults only. Some content may be disturbing to some readers.)
