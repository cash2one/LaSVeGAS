24851174:::::Angel of Everything:::::FIRST AMERICAN EDITION OF THE CONTROVERSIAL FRENCH NOVEL Bored with her life in a quiet, French coastal town and desperate for excitement, Emma sends out a prayer to the 'Angel of Happenings.'
