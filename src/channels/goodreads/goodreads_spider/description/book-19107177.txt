19107177:::::Louie the Llama Discovers Christmas:::::Curious to know what the holiday lights, carolers and shoppers are all about, Louie the Llama embarks on an exciting journey to discover the tender and true meaning of Christmas. Louie the Llama Discovers Christmas is part of the "Louie the Llama" series. It is an illustrated Children's Christmas story with a tender, Christian message conveying the true meaning of Christmas.
