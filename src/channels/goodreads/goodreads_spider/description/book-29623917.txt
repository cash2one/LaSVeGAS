29623917:::::I Dream of Yellow Kites:::::"When you realized your life-like dream wasn't real, you woke up. But when I realized my life was a dream, I didn't wake up." When Dahlia Adler sees her body lying twisted and crumpled on the ground below her, she wonders if it isn't all just a nightmare. But as things become clearer, Dahlia realizes it's all too real. She has to find her killer. And she has to move on.
