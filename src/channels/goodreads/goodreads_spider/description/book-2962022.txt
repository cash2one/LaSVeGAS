2962022:::::Around the World in Eighty Days:::::Jules Verne and his one-of-a-kind hero, Phileas Fogg take children on an action-packed, whirlwind race around the world. For as long as anyone can remember, Fogg s daily ritual has never varied by even a minute. Then, on a whim and a bet, he sets out to prove that he can span the globe and return to his club in London in only 80 days. Suddenly, his life is turned upside down, and every day offers an exciting new adventure. Kids will love it!
