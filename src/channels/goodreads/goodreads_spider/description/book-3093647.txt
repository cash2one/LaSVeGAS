3093647:::::Fairytale: a true story.:::::"Fairy Tale: A True Story" tells the tale of two little girls who set out to prove the existence of fairies. Grownups don't believe in the tiny sprites but they don't know where to look for them. If only the girls could photograph the fairies and show them to the world. Soon to be a major motion picture from Paramount, starring Harvey Keitel and Peter O'Toole.
