Filled with magic and wonder, The Magic Thief: Home transports readers to a land where a wizard s apprentice must battle both his enemies and his past to protect the world he knows. Despite successfully securing a balance between the competing magics of Wellmet, Conn is not happy. Duchess Rowan has promoted him to ducal magister, but the other wizards see him only as a thief. But something sinister is brewing, as magicians locus stones are being stolen and magical spells are going awry. As Conn faces old enemies and powerful magical forces, is he strong enough to save the city he calls home?
