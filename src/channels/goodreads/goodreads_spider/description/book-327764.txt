327764:::::The Final Quest:::::This panoramic vision was given to the author over a period of one year. It includes an unfolding of the last battle between light and darkness. A condensed version was printed in "The Morning Star Journal" as "The Hordes of Hell Are Marching--" it quickly became the most popular work we have ever printed, and has been circulated around the world. This is the complete vision, including Parts IV &amp; V, which have not been previously published.
