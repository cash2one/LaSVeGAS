36172487:::::The Shawshank Redemption:::::This modern film classic, an extraordinary tale of hope and survival inside a maximum security prison, follows the complex twenty-year relationship between two convicts who have little in common-except friendship. Based on the novella Rita Hayworth and The Shawshank Redemption by Stephen King, director/screenwriter Frank Darabont's film, starring Tim Robbins and Morgan Freeman, was nominated for seven Academy Awards , including Best Picture and Best Screenplay and has been named one of the 100 Best Films of All Time by the American Film Institute.
