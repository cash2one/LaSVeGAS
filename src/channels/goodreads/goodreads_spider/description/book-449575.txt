449575:::::The Satan Bug:::::To the outside world, the Mordon Labs existed solely for experiments in preventive medicine but in reality they were secret laboratories for the development of germ warfare. The most carefully hidden secret was the Satan Bug -- a strain of toxin so deadly that the release of one teaspoon could annihilate mankind. Late one night, the Mordon security officer was found murdered outside that lab. And the Satan Bug was missing...
