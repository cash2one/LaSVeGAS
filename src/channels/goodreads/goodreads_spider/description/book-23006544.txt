23006544:::::Button Goes to Hollywood:::::In volume one we enjoyed Button, a cute yellow puppy, as he attended doggy home school. In volume two, we followed Button on his first visit to Dr. Pig, where he learned that he could talk like his best friend and master, Buffy. Now, in this volume three, we will follow adorable Button as he participates in a doggy talent show. Winning first place takes him to Hollywood. Who did Button meet on his first airplane trip? A perfect story for young readers who won't be able to resist reading about this adorable golden puppy for themselves.
