10827240:::::Bang the Drum Slowly:::::Henry Wiggen, hero of The Southpaw , became the best-known fictional baseball player in America. Now he is back again in Bang the Drum Slowly , throwing a baseball "with his arm and his brain and his memory and his bluff for the sake of his pocket and his family." Also available in Bison Book editions are The Southpaw , It Looked Like For Ever , and A Ticket for a Seamstitch , the other three volumes in the Henry Wiggen series.
