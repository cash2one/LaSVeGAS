893609:::::There Are Men Too Gentle to Live Among Wolves:::::A modern classic with more than one million copies in print, There are Men Too Gentle to Live Among Wolves is an outstanding collection of poetry. Of this book, Kavanaugh says: "This is a book born in my heart, born in the pain of ending one life and beginning another . . . in the excitement of finding life's meaning." Now in its 56th printing.
