13411920:::::A Tale of Two Vampires:::::Time isn t always on a vampire s side Iolanthe Tennyson has had a very bad year due in part to the very bad men in her life. So, she s accepted her cousin s invitation to spend the summer in Austria indulging in her photography hobby. There, rumors of a haunted forest draw Iolanthe into the dark woods and into the eighteenth century Nikola Czerny is a cursed man, forced by his half-brothers to live forever as a Dark One. But his miserable existence takes an intriguing turn when a strange, babbling woman is thrown in his path. Iolanthe claims to know Nikola s daughter three hundred years in the future. She also knows what fate in the form of his murderous half-brothers has in store for him. If only she knew the consequences of changing the past to save one good, impossibly sexy vampire
