7434464:::::The Passing of Mother Mary:::::A Catholic priest helps a child named Brownlow break free from her abusive aunt, Mary Catherine. But Mary Catherine would do anyting to regain her control, especially when she discovers that the child has an inheritance worth millions. She goes after Brownlow with the help of her ruthless father who was just released from prison. Brownlow is now given only one chance to begin a new life without Mother Mary, Can she do it? With Brownlow's faith and courage comes the end of the pain that tormented a town, a family, and a child without a name.This tale will inspire all to hold onto their faith to the very end. A dramatic modern day 'Oliver Twist' story that will be hard to put down.
