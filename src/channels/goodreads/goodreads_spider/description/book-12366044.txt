It has been combined as the new book 1 in the Rescue Me Saga as Masters at Arms &amp; Nobody's Angel. (On Goodreads at https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/2 ... and on Amazon as ASIN B00GR5990E.) MASTERS AT ARMS begins the journey of three men, each on a quest for honor, acceptance, and to ease his unspoken pain. Their paths cross at one of the darkest points in their lives. As they try to come to terms with the aftermath--forging an unbreakable bond--will they ever truly become masters of their own fates? Or would fate become masters of them?
