9587330:::::Diaries of an Emotional Prostitute:::::Delilah Hutchens was an ordinary girl; whose only struggle was determining if God was real. But after her mother's sudden abandonment and her father's love affair with alcohol, Delilah's ordinary home turned upside down, forcing her to denounce God and turn to emotional prostitution. When her first love, J.B. doesn't seek the same kind of fulfillment, her emotional rampage spins out of control. Delilah's story brings real issues to the forefront and strips fairytales we've been fed of happily ever after By the end of the novel, you will discover one of two facts: 1) You KNOW an emotional prostitute; or 2) You ARE an emotional prostitute.
