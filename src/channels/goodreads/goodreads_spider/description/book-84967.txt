84967:::::Family Story Collection:::::Disney celebrates the magic of growing up with this special anniversary story collection. Adapted from seventy-five years of classic Disney films, each story in this collection imparts a specific message to today's children. "A Fair Fight" shows how Peter Pan keeps his word, even in the midst of a perilous fight with Captain Hook. In "Born to Be King", Simba learns that he can't change his past, but he can improve his future. And in "Who Could Love a Beast?", Belle realizes that a gruff exterior can hide a tender heart.
