18123583:::::The Marriage Merger:::::Her sisters have found wedded bliss with their wealthy,wonderful dream men, but not Julietta Conte. She s stayed on terra firma as top executive of the family s corporation, La Dolce Famiglia bakery. Work is her passion, and her trendy Milan apartment her sanctuary . . . until Sawyer Wells, a masculine masterpiece in a suit, lures her out of hiding with an irresistible offer: an exclusive partnership with his international chain of boutique hotels. Julietta s been burned before and trusting her brother-in-law s friend, whose powerful gaze alone has her rethinking the best use of a conference room, is the riskiest proposition. But with a once-in-a-career chance to take the bakery global, will she mix stone-cold business with red-hot seduction?
