2113443:::::Believing:::::After her tumultuous summer in Lily Dale, Calla has decided to stay, hoping to unearth more about her mother s untimely death. As she starts school at Lily Dale High and begins to explore her relationships with Jacy and Blue, her visions begin to occur with greater urgency. There may be a killer on the loose, and he may be after Calla for her role in solving his first victim s disappearance. Now that Calla believes in her ability, can she learn to use it properly before it leads her into more danger? In this thrilling sequel to Lily Dale: Awakening , readers will find an even larger dose of mystery, suspense, and romance that will keep them coming back to Lily Dale.
