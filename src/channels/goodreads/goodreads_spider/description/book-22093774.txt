22093774:::::One Missing Link:::::THIS IS FOR MATURE AUDIENCES ONLY! HORROR, THRILLER, SUSPENSE ROMANTIC NOVELLA. Sophia Martin moves to a Cibolo, Texas with her family as her father is a pilot in the Air Force. She is a military brat and used to the life. When she starts Colby HS, there is big news that two girls have gone missing. People are fascinated by how much she looks like them. Sophia meets Damon Oliver, also a senior, who dotes on her until she falls for him. That s when she becomes part of the search for the missing girls. She is the link to bring them home. Or perhaps the next victim? She hurries to find her future abductor before he finds her.
