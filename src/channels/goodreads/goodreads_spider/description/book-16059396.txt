16059396:::::The Girl Who Was Supposed to Die:::::Take her out back and finish her off. She doesn t know who she is. She doesn t know where she is, or why. All she knows when she comes to in a ransacked cabin is that there are two men arguing over whether or not to kill her. And that she must run. In her riveting style, April Henry crafts a nail-biting thriller involving murder, identity theft, and biological warfare.
