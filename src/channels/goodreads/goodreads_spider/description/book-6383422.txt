6383422:::::Revolutionary Empire: The Rise of the English-Speaking Empire from the Fifteenth Century to the 1780s:::::A new edition, previously published in 1981 by Cape, this is the story of the early phases of English and British expansion throughout the world. Calder interweaves English, Irish, Scottish and colonial events into a single pattern. He concerns himself with social and intellectual history, as well as with political and economic developments.
