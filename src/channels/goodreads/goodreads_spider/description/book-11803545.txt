11803545:::::Hot Dogs Under the Dakota:::::In South Africa in the 1950s, a young boy struggles to realise his individuality while dealing with an abusive grandfather and a capricious mother. Set against the background of emerging Apartheid with its vicious consequences, this gripping story of cruelty, duplicity, love and human failings unfolds to culminate in murder. Told with insight, compassion and humour it is A Must Read!
