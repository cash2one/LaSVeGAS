13533758:::::Foretold: 14 Tales of Prophecy and Prediction:::::Have you ever been tempted to look into the future? To challenge predictions? To question fate It's human nature to wonder about life's twists and turns. But is the future already written - or do you have the power to alter it? From fantastical prophecies to predictions of how the future will transpire, Foretold is a collection of stories about our universal fascination with life's unknowns and of what is yet to come as interpreted by fourteen of young adult fiction's brightest stars.
