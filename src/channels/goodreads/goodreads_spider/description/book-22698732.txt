22698732:::::Overshadowed Hope:::::Life is a struggle for Missy Highwater, a twenty-four year old private school teacher. She struggles with her class room full of students who don t respect her. Her fifteen year old pregnant sister has strained Missy s relationship with her mother, and her grandfather just received a diagnosis of Alzheimer s disease. Life is crashing down around Missy like a pile of bricks and her biggest struggle of all is her eating disorder. Take a trip with Missy as she tries to navigate through the labyrinth that is her family and tries to find the strength to overcome it all.
