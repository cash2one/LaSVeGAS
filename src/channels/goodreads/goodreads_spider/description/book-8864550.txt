8864550:::::Can You Survive the Zombie Apocalypse?:::::Inside these pages lies unspeakable horror. Bloodsplattering, brain-impaling, flesh-devouring horror. You ve probably read your fair share of zombie stories. But this time it s different. No longer can you sit idle as a bunch of fools make all the wrong moves. All hell is about to break loose and YOU have a say in humanity s survival. You have choices to make. Moral dilemmas. Strategic decisions. Weapons. Vehicles. Will you be a hero? Or will you cover your own ass at all costs? Can you withstand the coming hours, days, weeks, and months? Or will you die amidst the chaos and violence of a zombie uprising? Or, worst of all, will you become one of them?
