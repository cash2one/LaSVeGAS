17668133:::::Writing About Villains:::::Create the villain your novel deserves: a fiend whom your readers will love to hate and can never forget. Instead of cardboard evil-doers with evil laughter and stinking breath, you will develop villains with personality, ideals, feelings and conflicts. They will challenge your heroes, chill your readers, and give your novel excitement and depth.
