122884:::::Ragweed:::::The first book in the beloved Poppy series by Newbery Medal winning author Avi, with illustrations from Caldecott Medal winning artist Brian Floca A mouse has to do what a mouse has to do. Ragweed is determined to see the world. He leaves his family and cozy country home and sets off by train for the big city. What wonders await him: music, excitement, new friends...and cunning, carnivorous cats! Silversides is the purring president of F.E.A.R. (Felines Enraged About Rodents), a group dedicated to keeping cats on top, people in the middle, and mice on the bottom. Can Ragweed and his motley yet musical crew of city nice Clutch, Dipstick, Lugnut, and Blinker band together to fight their feline foe?
