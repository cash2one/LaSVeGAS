2659997:::::Stolen Innocence: My Story of Growing Up in a Polygamous Sect, Becoming a Teenage Bride, and Breaking Free of Warren Jeffs:::::Stolen Innocence is the gripping New York Times bestselling memoir of Elissa Wall, the courageous former member of Utah s infamous FLDS polygamist sect whose powerful courtroom testimony helped convict controversial sect leader Warren Jeffs in September 2007. At once shocking, heartbreaking, and inspiring, Wall s story of subjugation and survival exposes the darkness at the root of this rebel offshoot of the Mormon faith.
