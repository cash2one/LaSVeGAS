64634:::::Akiko and the Intergalactic Zoo:::::Akiko starts Grade 5, leader of the safety patrol. From planet Smoo, the King, grateful for his son's rescue last year, sends Akiko, Spuckler Boach, Gax, Poog, and Mr Beeba on a paid vacation to planet Quilk, home of the Intergalactic Zoo. Creatures wander in every shape, color, and size, hard to distinguish from visitors. How will Akiko escape when the zookeeper adds her to his personal collection?
