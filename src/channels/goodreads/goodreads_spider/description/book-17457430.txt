17457430:::::The Secret History of a Teenage Vampire:::::Larissa Kinley is a fully armed Operator for Department 19, a secret branch of the British government dedicated to saving us all from the supernatural. She s also a vampire. And a teenage girl. When the 17-year-old survivor of a vampire attack is brought to the Department s secret base, Larissa sees the chance to find out what a normal life is like, the kind of life she could have had if things had gone differently. As the two girls talk, Larissa finally finds the courage to tell someone her deepest secrets, and the terrible things she has done to survive as a creature of the night. But when the past catches up with you, sometimes it has fangs Included in the World Book Day 2013 App.
