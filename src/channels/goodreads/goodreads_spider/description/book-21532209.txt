21532209:::::Elfquest: The Original Quest Gallery Edition:::::In 1978, Wendy and Richard Pini first published what would become one of the most revered classic fantasy epics in comics. Today, ElfQuest continues to capture the imaginations of readers of all ages, as Chief Cutter and his Wolfriders battle savage humans, forge lasting alliances, and discover strange new lands in this ultimate fantasy adventure! This one-of-a-kind gallery edition collects the first five issues of The Original Quest, Fire and Flight , in their gorgeous original glory. Each page is carefully scanned from Wendy Pini's original art to capture every stroke and detail. At 12 1/8" by 17", it's as close to holding her original art as a fan can get.
