865540:::::Matter and Memory:::::One of the major works of an important modern philosopher, Matter &amp; Memory investigates the autonomous yet interconnected planes formed by matter &amp; perception on the one hand &amp; memory &amp; time on the other. Henry Bergson (1859-1941) was awarded the Nobel Prize in 1927. His works include Time &amp; Free Will, An Introduction to Metaphysics, Creative Evolution &amp; The Creative Mind.
