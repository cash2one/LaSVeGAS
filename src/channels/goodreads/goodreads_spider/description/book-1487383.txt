1487383:::::Diana: The Last 24 Hours:::::The last day of Diana, Princess of Wales remains shrouded in secrecy and speculation. Amid fresh allegations of cover-ups, witness manipulation and even conspiracy to murder, the watching awaits -- and demands -- the truth behind the most tragic death of our century.Now, thanks to access to previously undisclosed audio surveillance tapes, Allan Silverman can reveal amazing details of Diana and lover Dodi's last 24 hours of life. In Diana: The Last 24 Hours Silverman finally details the doomed Princess' hourly movements, and her intimate interactions with Dodi leading up to her Paris car crash, and pierces the complex web of deceit, accusation and counter-accusation spun by both media and monarchy since that fatal night.
