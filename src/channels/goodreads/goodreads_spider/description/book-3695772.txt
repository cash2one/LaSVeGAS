3695772:::::Chatham by Air:::::'Chatham by Air' is an 80-page coffee table book featuring the aerial photography of Chatham, Massachusetts by photographer Christopher Seufert. He is also the author of Chatham's very first photography book, Chatham Views. This special publisher's edition is signed by the author and includes a companion 40-minute Chatham by Air DVD, featuring 50 additional bonus photos plus 3 Chatham aerial videos. ISBN 978-1-60643-940-1
