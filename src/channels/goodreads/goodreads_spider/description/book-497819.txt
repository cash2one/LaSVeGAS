497819:::::Black Juice:::::In this extraordinary short story collection, human frailty is put to the test by the relentless forces of dark and light, man and beast. Each tale offers glimpses into familiar, shadowy worlds that push the boundaries of the spirit and leave the mind haunted with the knowledge that black juice runs through us all. Contents: Earthly Uses (2004) House of the Many (2004) My Lord's Man (2004) Perpetual Light (2004) Red Nose Day (2004) Rite of Spring (2004) Singing My Sister Down (2004) Sweet Pippit (2004) Wooden Bride (2004) Yowlinin (2004)
