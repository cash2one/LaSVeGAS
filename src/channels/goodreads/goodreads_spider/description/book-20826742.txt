20826742:::::Sacrifice:::::The tenth book in the Forever Series sees Liv s men struggling to live without her and their efforts to find her uncover some startling revelations. As they each suffer in their own ways, it draws them closer together, but as soon as Liv arrives back home with distressing news of her own, it blows them all apart again. Each one is determined to have her choose him and they will go to any lengths to ensure that happens, some claiming that a sacrifice has to be made. She is torn in all directions, and while still grieving her unexpected loss, she makes a decision that tears one of her men away from her, and another decision that could have catastrophic consequences for her down the road. But, this isn t all, when she is cornered she is forced into making a sacrifice against her will. But who or what will it be?
