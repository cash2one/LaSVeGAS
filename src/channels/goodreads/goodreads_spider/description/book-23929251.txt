23929251:::::Escaping The Wheel: A Novel:::::He's had enough . . . Rostam, an escaped convict obsessed with reincarnation and karma, seeks more than revenge. He wishes to end his time on the Wheel of Life. But first he must right the wrongs of countless past lives and obtain forgiveness from those who would continue to pull him back to Earth. In the process, lives are destroyed in order to be "saved". Not a believer? You will be . . . in the end.
