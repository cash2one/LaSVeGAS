22312293:::::Unveiled:::::The Ironskin Clan is still reeling from the events of the year before, still unable to fully recover from the defeat at the hands of Ose, the Archdemon of Insanity. All hope seems lost. Liz has grown despondent and unresponsive. She's lost the will to carry on the fight. Nevertheless, the war continues... The forces under Abaddon's banner have revealed themselves, but their exact numbers are still unclear. With an unknown amount of enemies, and the highest stakes of any battle, can Liz and her family prevent the end of the world that the demons all clamor for?
