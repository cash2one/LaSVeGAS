6043946:::::The 50th Law:::::In The 50th Law , hip hop and pop culture icon 50 Cent (aka Curtis Jackson) joins forces with Robert Greene , bestselling author of The 48 Laws of Power , to write a bible for success in life and work based on a single principle: fear nothing. With intimate stories from 50 Cent's life on the streets and in the boardroom as he rose to fame after the release of his album Get Rich or Die Tryin, as well as examples of others who have overcome adversity through understanding and practicing The 50th Law , this deeply inspirational book is perfect for entrepreneurs as well as anyone interested in the extraordinary life of Curtis Jackson.
