13595311:::::Cuentos drolaticos:::::Published between 1832 and 1837, these short stories constitute an extraordinary project of playful writing whose exuberant use of invented language caused a scandal in the literary world at the time. Complemented by the engravings of Gustave Dor , the stories purport to provoke the readers merriment and laughter through their mocking tone and ostensible immorality. Thanks to the cross references between the individual tales, the result is a collection of circular short stories that make up an interrelated and well-constructed whole.
