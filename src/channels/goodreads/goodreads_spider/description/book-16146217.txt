16146217:::::Rurouni Kenshin: Restoration, Vol. 1:::::A condensed retelling of the beloved samurai tale--one of the best-selling manga series of all time released in conjunction with a new live-action movie. During the violent upheaval of the Bakumatsu era, Hitokiri Battosai was a feared and ruthless assassin. But now that the Meiji Restoration has begun to heal the wounds of civil war, Battosai has taken up a new name and a new calling! He is now Himura Kenshin , a rurouni wanderer who has vowed to only draw his sword to protect those in need. But not everyone is pleased with Kenshin s new direction, and enemies from his dark past have vowed to bring him down! Reads R to L (Japanese Style) for teen audiences.
