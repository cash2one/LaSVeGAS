17700015:::::Champagne Toast:::::Note: Although these books are part of a series, they do not need to be read in order. Kate Armour and Evan Maxwell fell hard and fast for each other while attending college. Their once passionate and loving relationship ended when old scars eventually tore them apart. Two years after their breakup, they're each struggling to piece together what went wrong in an attempt to move forward. Kate, a no-nonsense bartender, must face her past while attempting to focus on her future. Evan is stifled by his bitter heart, which threatens each and every relationship he attempts after walking away from Kate. Will life offer them a second chance at love or are old patterns simply too hard to break?
