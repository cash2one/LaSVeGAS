27844439:::::The Eternal: Guardian of Light:::::Auren, a shape shifting shaman from thousands of years in the past, pursues the Devian which killed his family and fled to the future. Knowing he must face the most powerful creature from the lower dimensions to free his brother's soul. Setting a trap for Auren in the future, desiring Auren's power, the Devian uses the woman Auren loves to force him to comply and sacrifice his soul and powers. Auren must find a way to free his woman and save his own soul. If he fails, earth and all the higher dimensions will be overrun by the Devian Hordes that desire to conquer all dimensions. Can Auren survive the epic battle with the King of the Devians and his most trusted General.
