3409945:::::The Woppapotamus from Titan:::::Woppapotami are gentle, thick-skinned, mostly without a care in the world like kind-hearted Wally Woppapotamus. But the arrival of an alien space probe turns Wally's comfortable existence upside down when he is given an awesome task by his leader. It's up to him to save his own planet from invaders and disaster.
