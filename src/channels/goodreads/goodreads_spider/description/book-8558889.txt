8558889:::::Raider's Heart:::::Steal away to the Old South with Marcia Gruver s Backwoods Brides series. Two brothers one violent and one gentle belong to a family of bandits. While Duncan McRae can hardly stomach their raids, his brother Hooper revels in the violence perpetrated by the band of thieves. When they meet mild-mannered Dawsey Wilkes, the competition steps up a notch as the brothers vie for her hand. Which marauder will steal Dawsey s heart? Meanwhile, Dawsey holds the key to Dilsey McRae s past. What will Dilsey do when she discovers the truth?
