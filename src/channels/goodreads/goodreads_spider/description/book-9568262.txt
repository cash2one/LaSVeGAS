9568262:::::Clean Up After Me, I'm Irish: A Cheap Degenerate's Guide To Buffalo Bars:::::A 2 year look at Buffalo bars through the eyes of author and columnist Tom Waters, this book covers all of the writer's misadventures in Night Life magazine from 2005 to 2007. Before Tom Waters was a world renowned author and notorious goofball, he drank whiskey, and plenty of it. Clean Up After Me, I'm Irish: A Cheap Degenerate's Guide To Buffalo Bars shows the reader this tawdry, burpy, bourbon soaked side of the author in full detail, warts and all. Strap on your beer goggles and get ready for wacky antics!
