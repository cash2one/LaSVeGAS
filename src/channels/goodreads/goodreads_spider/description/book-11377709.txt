11377709:::::The Contract:::::The goddess of the hunt carries an entirely different meaning in Diana s world. A contract assassin for her father Jupiter, Diana trades her traditional gowns for a crossbow and vowed to fulfill her duties by eliminating anyone who threatened the pantheon. But when she receives a contract to kill the sexy Cole Hunter, the heat between the two ignites. Diana develops feelings she s never experienced and is faced with the decision to give her father, Jupiter, the human s heart, or give Cole hers. The race is on to protect him from Jupiter s wrath, before she loses him forever.
