23269505:::::How Strong Are You, Little Wave?:::::Big Wave brags to Little Wave day and night! Big Wave thinks he is the king of the sea. Little Wave is so sweet and gives a listening ear to his friend, Big Wave, who usually talks big about how strong he is or how pretty his smile is, or something about his good deed of the day. One day, something happens and Little Wave is missing. Big Wave frantically searches the ocean for him. Will he find his little wave-y friend? Will he have a change of attitude? Has Big Wave lost his best friend? "THIS STORY IS SUITABLE AS A READ-ALOUD BOOK FOR PRESCHOOLERS OR A SELF-READ BOOK FOR BEGINNER READERS."
