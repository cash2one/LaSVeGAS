18604211:::::Scent of Fear:::::Dashing Irishman Flynn Doherty sweeps Evie Harris off her feet in a whirlwind holiday romance. His passion overwhelms her though and she vanishes from his life without a trace. Years later they meet again, only Flynn's heart has become embittered and Evie must decide if their love is worth fighting for or if they would be better to remain apart. Furious at her rejection, Flynn pursues Evie relentlessly, determined to work her out of his system so he can move on with his life. The more he tries to hold onto her, the further she slips from his grasp.What is Evie running from? What is the secret she hides behind? Can Evie's love heal Flynn's bitter heart or will their tempestuous relationship end up tearing them both apart?
