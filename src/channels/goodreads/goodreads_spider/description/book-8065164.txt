8065164:::::The Dazzle Disaster Dinner Party:::::Sassy and her sparkle sack are back -- this time in a kitchen caper! Sassy wants to cook a fancy meal and it turns out to be a disaster. Who knew stuff in a sack could save a dinner party?! Well, when you re Sassy Simone Sanford, a little ingenuity goes a long way.
