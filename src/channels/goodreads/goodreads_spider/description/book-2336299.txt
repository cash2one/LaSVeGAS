2336299:::::Girls of Grace Q & A:::::In each Girls of Grace conference, the members of the popular Christian group Point of Grace deal with the hot topics facing young women today. Denise, Heather, Shelley and Leigh, along with their friend and director of Mercy Ministries, Nancy Alcorn, openly talk about family, sex, dating, friendships, and more. Blending their own insights with biblical instruction, they offer a fresh, contemporary style and helpful advice and encouragement.
