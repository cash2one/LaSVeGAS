8243678:::::Heart of the Family:::::In this unusual story of a family and the strangers it takes to its heart, Elizabeth Goudge reintroduces us to the Eliots, a clan we grow to love and cherish. "The author's exquisite portrayal of children, grownups, animals and the English countryside gives it the refreshing charm for which she is famous." (Library Journal) A sequel to PILGRIM'S INN, THE HEART OF THE FAMILY is the third in Goudge's trilogy about the Eliot family.
