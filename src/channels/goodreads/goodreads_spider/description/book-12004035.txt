12004035:::::Bluegrass State of Mind:::::McKenna Mason is on the run, leaving her luxurious NYC lifestyle behind. She finds a home in beautiful Keeneston, KY where she starts her new job as an Assistant DA. She finds more than she bargains for when reuniting with a teenage crush, Will Ashton. Will's horse farm has seen its share of trouble leading up to the Derby. Can Will and McKenna work together to cross the finish line in time?
