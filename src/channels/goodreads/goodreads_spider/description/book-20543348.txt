20543348:::::The Stolen Child:::::Now that she has discovered her true heritage, Ramsey Wilder is ready to end the war between elves and Element fairies. But when King Vortigern finds a way to put restrictions on magical traveling, Ramsey is forced to travel through each Magical Realm before crossing into the Golden Fairy Realm and requesting aid from Queen Titania. Wary but determined, Ramsey and her companions set off on a journey filled with enchanting new experiences.
