22920732:::::Code Name Verity:::::On October 11th, 1943, a British spy plane crashes in Nazi occupied France. Its pilot and passenger are best friends. but only one of the girls has a chance at survival. Arrested by the Gestapo, "Verity" is given a choice: reveal her mission or face a grisly execution. They'll get the truth out of her. Only, it wont be what they expect.
