809248:::::The Twig Trilogy : Includes Beyond the Deepwoods, Stormchaser & Midnight Over Sanctaphrax:::::A fantastic hardcover edition of the Twig trilogy of THE EDGE CHRONICLES, bringing together Beyond the Deepwoods; Stormchaser; and Midnight Over Sanctaphrax. Beyond the Deepwoods is the first title in the Twig trilogy and tells the story of young Twig s quest to discover his roots, not among the trees in Deepwoods, but high in the skies. Stormchaser is the second title in the Twig trilogy and follows Twig who is now a crew-member on his father s sky pirate ship, Stormchaser. Initially, Twig s dreams of exploration seem fulfilled. But a much higher destiny awaits him. In Midnight Over Sanctaphrax, the third title in the trilogy, Twig, now a sky pirate captain, is the only one who can save the Edgelands from disaster. But the task is daunting: his crew lost and his memory shattered. Can he make the journey back into the Deepwoods to recover his memory and take action?
