3457715:::::Bliss:::::Lauren Myracle brings her keen understanding of teen dynamics to a hypnotic horror story of twisted friendship. When Bliss s hippie parents leave the commune and dump her at the home of her aloof grandmother in a tiny Atlanta neighborhood, it s like being set down on an alien planet. The only guide na ve Bliss has to her new environment is what she s seen on The Andy Griffith Show. But Mayberry is poor preparation for Crestview Academy, an elite school where the tensions of the present and the dark secrets of the past threaten to simmer into violence. Openhearted, na ve Bliss is happy to be friends with anyone. That s not the way it has ever worked at Crestview, and soon Bliss is at the center of a struggle for power between three girls two living and one long dead.
