22267178:::::That's Who I Am:::::Jared Winters s life has been little more than a collection of moments where he didn t think before opening his mouth. Always a bit different, he s learned to accept an existence on the outside looking in. But one day, his mouth lands him in a position that nearly costs him his life. Only the timely intervention of Sophie, his twin Conner s ex-girlfriend, saves him. Siblings Sophie and Donovan Carrigan step in and offer support, comfort, and a place to call home. Under their care, Jared begins to heal and learns to trust just enough to act on his attraction to Donovan. He just hopes it s worth the risk.
