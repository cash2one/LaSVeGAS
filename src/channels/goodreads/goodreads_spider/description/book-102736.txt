102736:::::A Day in the Life of Africa: Photographed by the World's Leading Photojournalists on One Day:::::This epic collection is a one-day digital snapshot of the entire African continent. One hundred top photojournalists, including James Nachtwey and Sebastio Salgado, trek to 53 nations, shooting images from Cairo to the Cape of Good Hope. Photographs ranging from Namibia's San bushmen to the hip Lagos music scene capture a rich tapestry of African life lived on a single day. All publishing profits from the book will be used to fund AIDS education programs in Africa.
