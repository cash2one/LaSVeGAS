1724928:::::Saturnin:::::Zdenek Jirotka's famous humorous novel was first published in 1942 and since then, there have been more than 15 Czech editions. Over sixty years later, English-speaking readers can become acquainted with Jirotka's novel, whose main hero is the legendary faithful servant Saturnin, fighting with aunt Katerina and her son Milou . The book is accompanied by original colour illustrations by the Czech painter Adolf Born.
