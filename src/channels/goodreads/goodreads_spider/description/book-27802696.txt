27802696:::::Highland Angels:::::Anna MacKay fears the MacLeods. Andrew MacLeod fears love. Anna, angry with her brother, took a walk to cool her temper. She had no intention of venturing so close to MacLeod territory until she saw a wee lad fall through the ice. Anna, risked her life to save his beloved child. Now there is a chance to end the generations old hate and fear between their clans. Fate connects them. The desire for peace binds them. Will a rival tear them apart?
