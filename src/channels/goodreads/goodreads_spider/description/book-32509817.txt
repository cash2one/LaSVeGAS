32509817:::::Study Me:::::Work, Study, eat, and repeat. For the life of a pre-med student Marley Murphy has her work cut out for her. To make matters worse, her professor Houston Dale is one of the hardest teachers around. His sole purpose is to make each student suffer. When Marley is asked to be his assistant for the semester forbidden sparks will fly between them. Houston Dale hates teaching Anatomy. With a secret past he won t let anyone get too close. But, when his assistant lives across an alleyway and he can see all the naughty things she does in her bedroom at night, Houston can t contain himself. Forced to choose between his past and present, will Houston be able to be strong enough to withstand the temptation of his student? This is a forbidden Professor/Student affair. Get ready as Houston and Marley heat up the pages and your hearts.
