5546:::::The Feynman Lectures on Physics:::::The revised edition of Feynman's legendary lectures includes extensive corrections and updates collated by Feynman and his colleagues. Thus, this 4 volume set is the complete and definitive edition of The Feynman Lectures on Physics. Packaged in a specially designed slipcase, this 4 volume set provides the ultimate legacy of Feynman s extraordinary contribution to students, teachers, researches, and lay readers around the world.
