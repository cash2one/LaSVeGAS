25806563:::::His Heart Her Soul: Soul Series 1:::::Stormy has had visions of random people all her life, but she's never told anyone. Rain has been around for a long time. As the Alpha wolf shifter of a pack he has seen a lot of crap, but when that crap turns into a storm he had to walk away from the packs only home in Texas. When he finaly comes back he meets Stormy. Stormy really likes Rain, but can she trust him when push comes to shove? Rain doesn't know if he can get over his past to be with Stormy, but he has to save her when the other shoe hits the fan. Can he do it and maybe find love along the way?
