30157940:::::Shadow of a Human:::::Invincibility is fiction. Two years have passed since Nathaniel Runnels was abducted by a vampire and offered the choice between dying or risking his life to save humanity. In that time, he's killed, seen angels up close and learned that therians are never to be called werewolves. For a time, he thought new abilities meant he could not be defeated. That was also fiction. In the second installment of the Human Cycle, Nathaniel realizes that everything comes with a price. Shadow of a Human continues his path of discovery and he'll endure any pain to find the truth that he hungers for.
