10766057:::::My Name Is Mina:::::Mina's a rebel. She can't be controlled and she won't fit in. People say she's weird. Some say she's just crazy. But all she wants is to be free, to be happy, and to be herself. One night, as she sits in the moonlight, she picks up an empty notebook, and begins to write. And here is her journal, Mina's life in Mina's own words: her stories and dreams, experiences and thoughts, her scribblings and nonesense, poems and songs. Her vivid account of her vivid life.
