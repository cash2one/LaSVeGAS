4335732:::::1892:::::In 1892 , critically acclaimed novelist Paul Butler plunges the reader into 19th century St. John s, its light and its shade . . . An obscure servant, Kathleen, yearns for her home in Ireland. A mysterious scientist, Dr. Glenwood, believes he can be the first to bring a new photographic discovery to the world. A stable hand, Tommy Fitzpatrick, battles inner demons as he tries to win Kathleen s heart. These collective struggles will soon erupt to change the fate of an entire city. Longlisted for the 2009 ReLit Awards.
