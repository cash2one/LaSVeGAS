5548850:::::The Day Jesus Rode Into Croydon:::::The Day Jesus Rode Into Croydon is a mystery with elements of comedy and thought provoking realism. Occurring in contemporary times entwined with the golden days of childhood in the 1980s the story takes in the everyday setting of a life wasted in English suburbia to escape and vengeance in far off lands, revealing dark secrets and hidden pasts along the way.
