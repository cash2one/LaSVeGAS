1695356:::::The Third Book of Swords:::::The third and final novel in 'The Book of Swords' trilogy. More stories of the twelve Swords continue with the Lost Swords series. The gods, the creators of the twelve Swords, realize their error in giving powerful Swords to humans. The humans, both good and evil, are ready to fight to the death to acquire and retain the Swords. With the Swords, new ideas and new dreams have entered the world. A change is taking place that threats the gods' very existence.
