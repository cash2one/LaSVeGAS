88385:::::Mrs. Kimble:::::The award-winning debut novel from Jennifer Haigh, author of Baker Towers , The Condition , and Faith , tells the story of Birdie, Joan, and Dinah, three women who marry the same charismatic, predatory, and enigmatic opportunist: Ken Kimble. Resonating with emotional intensity and narrative innovation reminiscent of Ann Patchett s Bel Canto , Barbara Kingsolver s The Poisonwood Bible , and Zora Neale Hurston s Their Eyes Were Watching God , Haigh s Mrs. Kimble is a timeless story of grief, passion, heartache, deception, and the complex riddle of love.
