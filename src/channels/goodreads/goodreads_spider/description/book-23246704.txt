23246704:::::The Blue Moon:::::The strong-headed and explorative twins, Fera and Mira, are finally old enough to join their respective tribes and become full-fledged members of society. But when one last adventure leads to the discovery of a mysterious scroll, there are some serious repercussions. Suddenly an impending threat of war hangs over Freckania, and when the girls realize it may be their fault, they'll do anything to stop it. Now they must solve cryptic clues and race against their enemies to find the first piece of the Arasha Crystal.
