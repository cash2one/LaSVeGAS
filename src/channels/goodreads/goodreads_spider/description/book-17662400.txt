17662400:::::Godhead:::::Ken Mooney brings the Greek gods to the present day in this exciting New Adult dark fantasy Olympus has fallen; new gods must rise. The gods have been betrayed by one of their own. Zeus is dead, and the city is under siege by the demonic forces of Kaos. Their only chance of survival is to abandon the city, buying time to regroup and rebuild. Thousands of years later, the children of the gods walk among us, protecting humanity from the demons. But not all of them know the secrets of the powers they have. The gods are no more, eliminated by their greatest enemy. And now, she wants to return home; no matter who stands in her way.
