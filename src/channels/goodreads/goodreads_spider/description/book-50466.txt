50466:::::Burning in Water, Drowning in Flame:::::An omnibus edition of four early volumes of poems: "It Catches My Heart in Its Hands", "Crucifix in a Death Hand", "At Terror Street and Agony Way", and "Burning In Water, Drowning In Flame" finally. Burning in Water, Drowning in Flame is poetry full of gambling, drinking and women. Charles Bukowski writes realistically about the seedy underbelly of life.
