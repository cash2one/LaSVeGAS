2411828:::::Philippa Fisher's Fairy Godsister:::::Philippa Fisher tries &amp; fails to summon a fairy, but is taken aback when Daisy, the new girl at school, announces that she is her fairy godmother - or godsister, since they are the same age. Daisy is not best pleased with Philippa, but she has a mission to fulfil &amp; is obliged to stick with her until she has granted her three wishes.
