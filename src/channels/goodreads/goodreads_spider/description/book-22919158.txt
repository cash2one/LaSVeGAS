22919158:::::Conflict:::::The eleventh book in the Forever series sees Liv learning to accept what was done to her, and the drastic consequences of those actions. She suffers a huge loss, but soon information is revealed that shocks her and throws her life completely off balance. As her men struggle to deal with the conflict in her mind and body, and the competition for her heart is at an all time high, she learns something that has been kept from her and her turmoil increases. Faced with an old flame who reappears very unexpectedly, promises that she can no longer keep, a great loss, an overwhelming gain and more than one secret that tumbles out, she feels like her life is finally just too much, even for her. Will she be able to regain her footing and carry on as normal, or are the changes just too much this time?
