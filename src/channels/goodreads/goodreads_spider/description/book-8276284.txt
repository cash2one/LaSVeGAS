8276284:::::The Wolven Moon:::::If you could start over... Would you... And if you did would it be... An elderly man who believes he has lost everything, gets an offer to start anew, but in return he has to give something of himself, a piece and in exchange he gets something darker....... This is his story, as he runs from his curse, of the people he meets, how he learns he might not have lost everyone he loved, and that not all monsters are evil... A blessing... or a curse...
