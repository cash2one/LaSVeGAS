1101665:::::The Last of the Crazy People:::::The Last of the Crazy People is Timothy Findley s first novel, the compelling story of an eleven-year-old boy s private world of bewilderment and conflict. His mother won t leave her room, his adored older brother is drinking, and his father is obsessed with the family s disintegration, but seems unable to fix it. Left to himself, Hooker broods on events -- and takes terrifying steps to end the confusion.
