320012:::::The Boys in the Band:::::" Boys can still hold its own . . . [Mart] Crowley's point is about how the humor is shaped and defined by the pain."- The New York Times The Boys in the Band was the first commercially successful play to reveal gay life to mainstream America. Alyson is proud to release a special fortieth anniversary edition of the play, which includes an original preface by acclaimed writer Tony Kushner ( Angels in America ), along with previously unpublished photographs of Mart Crowley and the cast of the play/film. Mart Crowley 's other plays include the autobiographical A Breeze from the Gulf (1973) and The Men from the Boys (2002).
