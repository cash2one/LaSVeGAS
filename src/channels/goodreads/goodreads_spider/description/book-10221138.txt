10221138:::::Love Letters:::::Bridal shop manager Chloe Rogers will soon marry Richard Foster so she thinks until suddenly, she bumps into her childhood sweetheart, Josh Goldman, whom she hasn t seen in thirteen years. The sparks between Chloe and Josh fly, but Richard provides safety, financial security. Should she follow her heart or her head? The answer comes in a surprise twist. While cleaning her attic, she stumbles upon love letters written to her estranged mother forty years ago from a man she loved. When Chloe secretly brings them together again and sees how much time they ve lost, she is challenged not to make the same mistake her mother made. Will Chloe opt for security or will she risk her heart and marry her true love?
