18003161:::::Heaven:::::Her name was Heaven. Her life was anything but. Heaven's mother Angel had died in childbirth, and from that moment her father turned against her. Her only allies, it seemed, were her four half-brothers and sisters. When their father, poor and desperate, sold them to strangers, Heaven was left alone and friendless. Her only hope was to reunite her family. But how? So begins the heartbreaking magical story of the Casteel family, continued in Dark Angel, Fallen Hearts, Gates of Paradise and Web of Dreams.
