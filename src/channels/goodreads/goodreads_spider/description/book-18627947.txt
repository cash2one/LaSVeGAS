18627947:::::Reap the Wind:::::You d think that being Chief Seer for the supernatural world would come with a few perks. But as Cassie Palmer has learned, being Pythia doesn t mean you don t have to do things the hard way. That s why she finds herself on a rescue mission skipping through time even though she doesn't entirely understand her dimension-bending new power. Rescuing her friend John Pritkin should have been an in and out kind of deal, but with the near-immortal mage s soul lost in time, Cassie has to hunt for it through the ages with Pritkin s demon dad in tow. He s the only one who can reverse Pritkin s curse, but with the guardians of the time-line dead set on stopping anyone from mucking about, Cassie will have to figure out how to get her friend back without ruffling too many feathers or causing a world-ending paradox or two...
