11433851:::::Chicken Soup for the Soul: Just for Teenagers: 101 Stories of Inspiration and Support for Teens:::::Teenage years are tough, but this book will help teens as they journey through the ups and downs of adolescence. The stories in this book serve as a guide on topics from the daily pressures of life and school to love, friendships, parents, and much more. This collection will encourage, inspire, and amuse teens, showing that, as tough as things can get, they are not alone!
