7564251:::::Secrets of the Sands:::::Cafad Scratha, a powerful desert lord with a persecution complex, believes everyone is lying to him. When his obsession collides with the king's efforts to rebuild the shattered realm, the orphaned street-thief Idisio and the king's emissary Alyea become pawns in their multilayered game. The secret world into which Idisio and Alyea are drawn will not only change their lives: it will change them. NOTE: This book was originally published through Mercury Retrograde Press; it is currently housed with ReAnimus Press.
