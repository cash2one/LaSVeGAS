15846228:::::Quinn's Undying Rose:::::Vampire bodyguard Quinn has lived the last 200 years as a playboy trying in vain to forget the only woman he has ever loved: his human wife Rose, who he believes to be dead. But Rose is very much alive. Now a vampire herself, she has lived in hiding from Quinn all of these years, having faked her death to hide from him a terrible secret one she knows would drive him to kill her. When a powerful and evil vampire threatens to destroy Rose s and Quinn s only living descendent, she has no choice but to come out of hiding and ask for Quinn s help. As Quinn struggles with the shock of Rose s reappearance, and they reunite to battle a common foe, they rekindle the flames of their past. But will the passion that reignites between them be enough to overcome the secret that separates them?
