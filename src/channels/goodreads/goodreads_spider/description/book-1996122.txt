1996122:::::Savage Destiny:::::A New York Times Bestselling Author Why would a wealthy Louisiana planter marry an Indian woman? townsfolk asked. Or raise two copper-skinned sons as though they were white? Only trouble would come of it. . . . Red Feather had grown up sensing a vital difference between his father's sons, and when his brother gambled away his mail-order bride, he knew that his younger sibling was obsessed with beating him.
