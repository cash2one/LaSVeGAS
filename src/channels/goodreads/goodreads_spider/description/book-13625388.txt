13625388:::::Ever:::::Seventeen-year-old Ever s love life has been on hold for the past two years. She s secretly in love with her best friend Frankie, and he s completely oblivious. Of course, it doesn't help that he s dead, and waking up to his ghost every day has made moving on nearly impossible. Frustrated and desperate for something real, Ever finds herself falling for her hot new neighbor Toby. His relaxed confidence is irresistible, and not just Ever knows it. But falling for Toby comes with a price that throws Ever s life into a whirlwind of chaos and drama. More than hearts are on the line, and more than Ever will suffer. Some girls lose their hearts to love. Some girls lose their minds. Ever Van Ruysdael could lose her soul.
