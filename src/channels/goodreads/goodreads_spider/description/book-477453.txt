477453:::::Born Pissed:::::Born Pissed is the culmination of three years of debauchery in the life of essayist Tom Waters. With his own brand of twisted, outspoken humor, the author fires off on smoking, strip clubs, old people, shopping, throwaway society, school shootings, cell phones, and other assorted joys and plagues in our modern world. His articles have appeared in The Buffalo News, Artvoice, and Night Life magazine.
