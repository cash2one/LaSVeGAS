16282616:::::Neil Flambe and the Toyko Treasure:::::World-class chef Neil Flambe isn t thrilled when his cousin Larry moves to Japan to work on an online manga comic book. Now who ll help him in the kitchen? But he finds a replacement in Gary the bike courier, and life, and the restaurant, moves on without Larry. That is, until the news that life may have really left Larry behind he s been lost at sea. Neil is devastated. But then he checks Larry s online manga. There s a subtle change in the plot, something Neil and Larry had discussed something only Neil would notice. Is this a cryptic message from beyond the grave or is Larry still alive? Determined to find out, Neil heads to Japan to solve his next mystery.
