334809:::::Roxaboxen:::::From two-time Caldecott Medal-winning illustrator Barbara Cooney and celebrated children s book author Alice McLerran comes Roxaboxen, a treasured story about the magic of a child s imagination. Marian called it Roxboxen. There across the road, it looked like any rocky hill nothing but sand and rocks, and some old wooden boxes. But it was a special place. And all children needed to go there was a long stick and a soaring imagination. A celebration of the transforming magic of the imagination. An original. ALA Booklist
