14427023:::::Rise of the Vampire:::::Hawaii born author, C.S. Revlis, takes readers on the first of several exciting journeys in Rise of the Vampire. Before Sarah Rusae passed away, she gave her son a crest which serves as the only clue to the identity of his father. Young Teo Rusae, and his best friend Beau, embark on a journey to find the truth. Their adventure will lead them into an era where the human race is in a battle for survival. Cast into the midst of the war between good and evil, Teo begins to discover who he is, and where he fits into the quickly changing world.
