2706866:::::In A Cold Sweat:::::Roy Glenn, the Master of Urban Suspense returns with the latest installment in the Mike Black Saga. In this fast-paced, action-filled novel, In a Cold Sweat we find underworld figure Mike Black still grieving after the brutal murder of his wife Shy. Haunted by the fact that the people behind her death are still breathing, Black vows to find those responsible and deal with them accordingly. Can Black exact revenge and still be a father to his infant daughter? Can he keep her safe from the men who want to do to her and to him, what they did to her mother? Find out in the pages of In a Cold Sweat.
