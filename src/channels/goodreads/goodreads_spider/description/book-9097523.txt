9097523:::::Libram of Incredible Tomes:::::This book is for Game Masters of Fantasy Roleplaying systems. It is a great supplement for novice and veteran Game Masters. With this book in hand, gone will be the boring and repetitive bookshelf descriptions of many a game. Instead, the Game Master will be able to give inventive and intriguing descriptions of shelf dressings, many of which are just ripe for sending your players careening off into yet another adventure. Excitement and dread await those players whose Game Masters are bold enough to use the Libram of Incredible Tomes.
