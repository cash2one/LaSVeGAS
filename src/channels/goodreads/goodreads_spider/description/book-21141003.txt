21141003:::::The Ferrymen:::::When a shootout with criminal mastermind Alexander Crowley, leaves his partner, Lincoln lying in a coma, Joe Buchanan begins to piece together his life. Searching for answers, Joe meets with Angie, an old friend who can talk to spirits, and she tells him of a new threat. Joe and Angie track down Crowley to a mysterious estate in New Orleans, where everything is not as it seems. As Joe digs deeper, Crowley has one more final trick up his sleeve, resurrecting his bloodline for one final battle with Joe. Joe will throw down the gauntlet, and prepare to make the ultimate choice. Kill the man responsible for Lincoln s coma, or capture the spirit responsible for their best friend s death. Either way, justice will be served.
