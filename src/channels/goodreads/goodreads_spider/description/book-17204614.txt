17204614:::::The Lady Series:::::Includes: Lady in Waiting Commanded by her grandfather to snare a wealthy husband, Anne Blanchemain enters the lavish court of Queen Elizabeth. She never suspects that handsome courtier Christopher Hollier has been sent to ruin her, not wed her. Christopher's desire for Anne soon sweeps them both toward betrayal and into a dangerous game that could change the fate of the queen herself. Lady in White A widow is forced to remarry and her betrothed is a mysterious recluse who conducts all business through his handsome agent. Suspicious and protective at first, the agent eventually falls in love with the spirited, courageous lady.
