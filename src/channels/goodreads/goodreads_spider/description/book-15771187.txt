15771187:::::Another Broken Wing, Flap Flap:::::A young guy ignores a barrage of coincidences in his life until an almost impossible fluke involving a Powerball lottery drawing opens up a pathway for him to discover why his mother vanished when he was born and why the peculiar truth about the rest of his family has been so well hidden. This is a story about synchronicity -- which illustrates that coincidences are not random but instead meaningful arrangements.
