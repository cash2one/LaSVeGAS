12777554:::::Light Through the Cracks:::::After surviving a horrific childhood, Lucy ends up on her own trying to find solid footing in her tumultuous life. Unfortunately, her early attempts at doing so leave her living in her car and knocked up by her drug dealer. Having a baby changes the way Lucy looks at herself and at life. It also allows her to redefine her concept of love. Light through the Cracks is a story of resilience, strength, and the ability to not only survive in a formidable world, but to find contentment and hope there, too.
