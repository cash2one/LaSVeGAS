16123515:::::The Double Edged Sword:::::L.J and her younger Sister Jo,had a harsh and traumatic childhood; they never felt they belonged. They were different. As L.J grew into a young woman she felt hopeless and unfulfilled; she hated life. Little did she know she was being watched, and her desperate need to belong as a child would lead her to a life changing reunion with Jo, a dark looming stranger and a surprise transformation. This would lead them both on a treacherous journey into the dark world of Vampires,Murderers.Bloodlust,Justice and Revenge.
