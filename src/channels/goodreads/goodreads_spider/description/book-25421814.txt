25421814:::::Wynter's War:::::Magic and adventure abound in this installment of the Keeper Saga. Nikki's nightmares are back and they are worse than ever. In a thick blanket of fog, a beautiful woman walks thru the Deadlands, bringing a dark, strong evil unlike anything the forest has ever seen, threatening to destroy all that the Keepers are bound to protect. As the guardian of Wynter's book, Nikki discovers a new magic held within its pages and a power that will affect good and evil magic alike. Hoping the secret to defeating Crow Woman lies in the book's knowledge, the Keepers join forces with an unlikely ally. Will Nikki and the Keepers be strong enough to win the battle over evil--or will they be lost to it forever?
