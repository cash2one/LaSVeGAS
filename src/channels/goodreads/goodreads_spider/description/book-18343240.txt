18343240:::::No Time Like Mardi Gras:::::A scandalous night and no one will ever know! Mardi Gras might be the best party in the world, but Jamie Vincent is spending it third-wheeling with her new roommate. So when gorgeous stranger Colin Raine offers to show her around, how can she say no? Soon Jamie's seduced by the hedonistic carnival and her delicious tour guide! Technically she's sworn off men, but for one night only, they can go crazy and no one will ever know . Until everyone in New Orleans finds out! Now that Jamie's secret is out, it looks like what happened at Mardi Gras might not stay at Mardi Gras, after all .
