18871985:::::An Unforgivable Secret:::::Hannah has a good life. A beautiful home, a loving husband, and a wonderful Amish community are only a few of her daily blessings. But she has carried a heavy burden for years: a secret that no one must know. When tragedy strikes, her secret threatens to be revealed, jeopardizing everything she s ever loved. Will Hannah be able to face her greatest fear and find God's purpose for her life?
