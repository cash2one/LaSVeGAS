6783155:::::The Song of the Whales:::::Michael s grandfather has a secret a secret that s almost too strange to share . . . When Michael moves to Israel, he leaves loneliness behind and steps into the light of his grandfather s magic. Like a sorcerer s apprentice, Michael learns how to blur the lines between dreams and reality when his grandfather hands down the most precious of gifts a gift that allows Michael passage into his grandfather s dreams. Written with a quiet simplicity that wins the reader over at once Uri Orlev writes in a style so sure and yet so unassuming that it is certain to linger in reader s minds long after turning the last page.
