9182654:::::For You They Signed:::::This thick study delves into the characters of the Signers of the Declaration of Independence. Arranged by state, this book may be used a year's worth of devotional character studies, as a complete resource for moms to use, an individual or group study, or as general inspirational reading. Each entry includes a list of facts, hand-drawn portrait, a reproduction of his signature, boxed quotes about the subject, a few pages that illustrate the life and beliefs of each signer, a timeline of their individual lives, and questions for discussion. 393 pages, hardcover.
