1707116:::::The Forgotten 500: The Untold Story of the Men Who Risked All For the Greatest Rescue Mission of World War II:::::In 1944 the OSS set out to recover more than 500 airmen trapped and sheltered for months by villagers behind enemy lines in Yugoslavia. Classified for over half a century for political reasons, this is the full account of Operation Halyard, a story of loyalty, self-sacrifice, and bravery.
