5989977:::::Be Mine:::::Just in time for Valentine's Day comes a funny, fresh novel from Sabrina James! Valentine's Day is fast approaching at North Ridge High! And this year, the school is sponsoring a "Most Romantic Couple" contest. Everyone wants to win including Jennifer, who's not going to let the fact that she doesn't actually have a boyfriend stop her! Romantic twists and turns and comic mishaps abound. Will the proper couples be reunited in time for the Valentine's Dance?
