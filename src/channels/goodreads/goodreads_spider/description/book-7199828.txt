7199828:::::Aphrodite the Beauty:::::Well-researched and true to the original myths, each volume in the Goddess Girls series addresses contemporary issues like friendships and relationships from a classically accurate and entertaining persepective. In Aphrodite the Beauty , Aphrodite, goddessgirl of love, must deal with jealousy after giving Athena a makeover. It doesn t seem fair that the godboys pay more attention to her friend when Aphrodite is supposed to be destined for love! She also copes with a crush from an unlikely source the nerdy Hephaestus (god of the smith) and learns that love comes in many forms.
