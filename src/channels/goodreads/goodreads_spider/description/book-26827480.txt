26827480:::::Saint Vampire:::::"Every human being on the face of the earth has a story to tell. And so does every monster. This is my story. My gift to you. My special warning......." So starts the SAINT VAMPIRE legend. A story that spans history, as the forces of heaven and hell wage war, using earth as their battlefield and our children as their captured pawns. The worlds of Vampires, Werewolves, Angels, Witches and Saints collide to provide a sometimes lusty and always entertaining thrill ride for the reader. When it comes to the SAINT VAMPIRE legend, expect the unexpected. If you like reading vampire stories, you're going to love reading this one. If you've never read a vampire story this is a great place to start. If you think you aren't interested in reading stories about vampires, I dare you to read the sample and not want more.
