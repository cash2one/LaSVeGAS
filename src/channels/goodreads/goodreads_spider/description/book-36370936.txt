36370936:::::The Talents of Bet:::::Sold into servitude at the age of fourteen, the orphaned Bet endures constant abuse from the mistress of a backwater farm. Despite her heinous treatment, she keeps her spirits high, for she has a secret she can talk with animals. With the aid of her friend Pooch-kin, they make adventures of the constant chores. While gathering wood, Bet finds a glimmering crystal in the sludge of a muddy creek. This chance discovery sets her on a course of real adventure; where she finds speaking with animals is only one of her many Talents.
