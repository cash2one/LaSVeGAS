3296447:::::The Awful Apple Orchard:::::Have some tasty, ghostly apples!!! Daniel and his little sister, Sara, are allowed to miss school for a two week vacation. While enjoying the view, their father mentions rumors that the local apple orchard and cider mill are haunted. Frightening things begin to happen. Daniel and Sara start to think the story was more than just a rumor. Worse, they have reason to doubt if anyone will believe them. . .and worse still, Sara may be part of the recipe for GHOSTLY APPLE SAUCE. . .
