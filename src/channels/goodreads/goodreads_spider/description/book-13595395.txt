13595395:::::Crossing the Line: The Outrageous Story of a Hockey Original:::::The autobiography of one of hockey s first rebels and a beloved member of the Big Bad Bruins, this book shares how Derek Sanderson s ferocious style helped lead the team to two Stanley Cup victories in the early 1970s. Living life in the fast lane, Sanderson grew his hair long, developed a serious drinking problem, and eventually found himself out of the league and prowling the streets for his next drink. In this autobiography, Sanderson comes clean on his life in hockey, the demons that threatened to consume him, and the strength and courage it took to fight his way back. Today a successful entrepreneur and speaker, Sanderson s incredible story is a must read for any fan of hockey.
