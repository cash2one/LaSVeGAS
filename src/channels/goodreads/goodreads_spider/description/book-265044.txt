265044:::::Rusty String Quartet:::::Second collection of work from Crimethinc's poet laureate. 332 (largely short) poems which run the gamut of incarceration, alienation, hope, despair, and all the barbs that society throws at us. The author is now out of prison, though much of this was written whille still inside. All encased in the usual lavish, beautiful CrimethInc production.
