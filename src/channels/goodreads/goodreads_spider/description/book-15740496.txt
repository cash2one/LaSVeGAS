15740496:::::Night Feast: A Vampire Novel:::::Lia is a beautiful vampire from the Victorian era, who finds a portal that enables her to enter modern day Portland, Maine. She satisfies her lust for human blood by taking the life of Elena Hudson, and while doing so she sees a vision of the boy, who her victim was in love with. Determined to meet him Lia sets up home in the 21st century, leaving her vampire family trapped in the 19th. She succeeds in meeting the boy, Jay and an illicit passion ignites between them. But Lia cannot control her desire for human blood forever, and she is afraid that Jay will discover that she is a vampire. Then the secret of her killing comes back to haunt her.
