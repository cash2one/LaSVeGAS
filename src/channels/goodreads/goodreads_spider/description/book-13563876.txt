13563876:::::The Legend of Goat Head Lodge:::::In the third book of The L &amp; L Mysteries, Leyla and Lucien are invited by their friend, Peter Lawson, to spend a week at Goat Head Lodge and Ski Resort. But they soon learn that it may be their last trip as the lodge may have to close due to financial problems. So, when Lucien and Leyla learn of a legend about a hidden treasure on the resort property, they resolve to find the treasure in hopes of helping keep the beloved lodge open. Hunting for clues, they navigate Billie the goat, avalanche control, and ski resort romances. Will the teens be able to find the treasure in time? Or is the lodge destined to be closed?
