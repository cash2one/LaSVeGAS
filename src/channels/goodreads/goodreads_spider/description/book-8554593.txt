8554593:::::Tales from the East:::::In this prequel to Imago Book One: Tales From the West, Nayla Treeborn is faced with a war that will decide the fate of all the free peoples. In a desperate bid to deliver word to the Elf King, she fights to survive the deadly elements in a strange land. This adventure recounts the defining moments in her life that forged her into a deadly warrior, a great captain and a legend amongst her people.
