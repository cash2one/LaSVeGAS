9942081:::::Mortal Kiss:::::How much would you sacrifice for just one kiss . . . When smouldering Finn and sexy Lucas arrive in Winter Mill, life starts to get very complicated for Faye McCarron. But two boys battling for her heart is just the start. Soon there's a dead body in the woods , a motorcycle gang on the prowl, and the snow just won't stop falling. Something evil is at work , and only Faye and best friend Liz can stop it. As Hallowe'en draws near they must uncover the dark and sinister secret . . . before it's too late. Love paranormal romance, werewolves and ethereal spirits? Then this book is for you!
