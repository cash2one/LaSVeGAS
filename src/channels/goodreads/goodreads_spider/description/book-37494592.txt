37494592:::::Angrily: A Short Story:::::Time heals, or does it? This is a fictional, yet personal story about finding peace during the darkest hour. Coming to terms with the past is a challenging and devastating process. When Gerry is sexually assaulted by a fellow Marine, her depression spirals down a self destructive path. Unable to form romantic relationships, she resorts to inflicting harm unto herself. Just as her anger is about to consume her whole, Gerry meets a young man, bringing new hope and meaning into her life, but will she be able to finally cope?
