19221972:::::Angel in the Ice:::::Megan Laughlin has a gift, or what seems like a curse at times to see angels. Judas, an evil angel enrolled in her high school, tags along with her youth group on their winter-break ski trip. While in the mountains of North Carolina, an approaching blizzard provides Judas the perfect cover to up the death count and gather more souls. Meanwhile, Sam fights his drug addiction, Seth discovers his gift, and Megan is confronted by Judas to deliver on her promise. Seemingly harmless temptations have life-changing consequences for the survivors at the resort. The first two books in the Amazon best selling Angel Series, Angel in the Shadows, Book 1 and Angel in the Storm, Book 2 have been optioned for a major movie. The Angel Series is a young adult series appropriate for age 12 on up.
