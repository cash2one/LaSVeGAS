2991589:::::An Unlikely Mormon:::::In this presentation, Glenn Beck tells an audience of nearly 7,000 about his conversion to The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. Glenn bears his testimony about home teaching, tithing, and the transforming power of the Spirit. He talks about his addiction to alcohol, the miracle of the Atonement, and the reality that God does answer prayers. In particular he acknowledges God's hand in how he met his wife, Tania, and in their family's spiritual quest for faith and religion.
