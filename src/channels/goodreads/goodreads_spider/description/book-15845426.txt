15845426:::::Kaguya Hime: A Tale of the Bamboo Cutter:::::Teen author of "The Venomous Monster," Melissa Mahinay presents you her first ever children's book for ages three and up. An old bamboo cutter finds a little girl inside of a glowing bamboo tree, and later adopts her as his own. Kaguya Hime is the daughter of the Moon. The Tale of the Bamboo Cutter happens to be one of Japan's oldest folktales known to man...being created way back in the 10th century.
