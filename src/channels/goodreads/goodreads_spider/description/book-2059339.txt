2059339:::::Ultra Cute, Vol. 9:::::The tale of our favorite love-crazy girls comes to end in this bittersweet final volume. Ami and Tamon's relationship is strained almost beyond repair, no thanks to Sana's devious ploys. Devasted, Ami takes her mind off her problems by playing in a heated basketball tournament against Tamon's school--and her love for Tamon depends on her team's victory or defeat! Will this be the end of Ami and Tamon? Or will it be a slam dunk?
