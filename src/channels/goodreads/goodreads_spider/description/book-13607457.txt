13607457:::::Wicked Game:::::A policeman is killed in a bomb blast, a second is gunned down in his own driveway. The Wicked Game has started. Former SAS and now Royalty protection officer Robert Finlay has had enough of the job s not-so-glamorous demands and opts to transfer to a local police station. A 'Wicked Game', where he is the target, is about to test his skills to the limit.
