38350519:::::The Ghost Years:::::Sometimes our simplest wishes can be the most difficult to attain.The Ghost Years is brutally honest and jarring, from it's immersive beginning right through to it's startling, unpredictable conclusion. It's a young man's attempt to pick up the pieces and start over only to be presented with greater obstacles than he could ever have envisioned. He is taken to unimaginable and devastating places, both physically and mentally. How much control does he really have over his fate? The Ghost Years goes deep beneath the surface through unflinching plot twists and hauntingly poetic prose.
