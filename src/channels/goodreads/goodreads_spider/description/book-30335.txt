30335:::::Demon in My View:::::Though nobody at her high school knows it, Jessica is a published author. Her vampire novel, Tiger, Tiger, has just come out under the pen name Ash Night. Now two new students have just arrived in Ramsa, and both want Jessica s attention. She has no patience with overly friendly Caryn, but she s instantly drawn to Alex, a self-assured, mysterious boy who seems surprisingly familiar. If Jessica didn t know better, she d think Aubrey, the alluring villain from her novel had just sprung to life. That s impossible, of course; Aubrey is a figment of her imagination. Or is he?
