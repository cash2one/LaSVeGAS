758784:::::Fuhrer-Ex: Memoirs of a Former Neo-Nazi:::::Ingo Hasselbach, whose parents were Communist Party members in East Germany during his childhood, has lived at both ends of the political seesaw. The question of how people reach a change of heart is a profound one; Hasselbach describes the external forces that led to his founding Germany's first neo-Nazi political party and the internal ones that led him away from it five years later with a surprisingly careful thoughtfulness and thoroughness that befits the issues at hand. based on: "Die Abrechnung" by Ingo Hasselbach (ISBN 3-351-02446-0)
