499409:::::My Soul Has Spoken:::::My Soul Has Spoken is a funny, warm, inviting, feel-good book that will bring new insight into your own pain and joy, along with the pain and joy of others. You'll laugh, cry, reflect and be blown away by the overwhelming feelings that will come alive in you after you read this book. Knowing you're not alone is powerful medicine that can be used in the first steps toward total healing. From death, to love, to the way we speak, to how we see others and ourselves-it is emotion-filled and power-packed with feelings and insights I have felt and seen. As you read this, you can't help but get a picture in your mind. It's an excellent gift to give to someone who's feeling down or just needs to know that she is not alone. It's meant to be shared and enjoyed.
