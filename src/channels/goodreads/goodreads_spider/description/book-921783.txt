921783:::::The Wary Transgressor:::::The sensuous splendour of Lago Maggiore, Milan and Florence is a setting for a ruthless murder plan... a sinister danse macabre, the first steps of which are taken when a young army deserter falls into the hands of a wealthy and cruel woman planning to kill her husband and step-daughter. But, unexpectedly, Fate intervenes and sets a deadly trap for - The Wary Transgressor...
