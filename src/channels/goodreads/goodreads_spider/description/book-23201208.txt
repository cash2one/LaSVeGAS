23201208:::::Redemption:::::In the final installment of The Redemption Series, Anna must face the challenges of retrieving the rest of the seals from the Princes of Hell. As her relationship with Lucifer grows stronger, she learns more about not only her father s past but also her own. Helena uses her growing influence to strengthen her dominion over Hell and tempt Anna s dark side into joining forces with her. Malcolm does everything within his power to help the love of his life deal with the transformations the seals are thrusting upon her, but he knows, in the end, that she is the only one who can decide her own fate. Through many trials and tribulations, Anna learns that the choices she makes can t always be so black and white. Sometimes, extreme circumstances force you to choose what s most important in life and who it is you really want to be.
