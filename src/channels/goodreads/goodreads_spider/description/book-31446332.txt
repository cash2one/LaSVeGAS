31446332:::::Wicked Meets Bad Boy:::::Nyla Laine took the saying To get over a man, you get under another one to heart. The day she found out her high school sweetheart was a lying cheater, Nyla did not stay down. She went out and had the most amazing night of her life. Isaac Sousa needed a night out. At the top of his game in business world, he should have been enjoying all life had to offer. Instead he spent most nights sitting by his mother s bedside. Meeting Nyla was an unexpected surprise and helped to get his mind off of his troubles. Now a girl who wants to be a little wicked indulges the bad boy she never knew she wanted.
