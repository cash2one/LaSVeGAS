11492014:::::Fractured:::::The Fateful Trilogy continues with Fractured. No, it s not called Fractured because Ethan and Danielle break up...in case you were wondering. They had thought they d escaped most of the danger surrounding them, when in truth they ve made it worse by unintentionally sparking a civil war amongst vampires. Many vampires want to protect them for discovering the cure to the curse binding their kind. However many more want to destroy them for that same discovery. Ethan s and Danielle s struggle for survival intensifies while they sink deeper into the midst of this danger. The romantic fairytale bliss they once had will soon fracture under the pressure of the curse....
