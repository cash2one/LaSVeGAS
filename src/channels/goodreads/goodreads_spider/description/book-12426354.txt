12426354:::::Endless Seasons:::::Endless Seasons is a collection of poems about oil, the Niger Delta, and emotions. Oppression, pain, corruption, exploitation and injustice are themes that run through the poems. There is the recurrent theme of female subjugation as well as the deep personal struggles of the African woman. In diverse ways, the poems poignantly portray the sufferings of women, men, and children of Africa.
