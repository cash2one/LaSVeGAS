26098978:::::The Elementals:::::Eighteen years before the events of Run to Earth, the Elementals lived in harmony together in the City of Marwa. But the youngest of the Elementals, Alex Adams, has a secret. One he s had to keep from his own brother, Christopher, because he knows Chris would never tolerate it. But with Alex soon coming into his full powers, he knows time s running out to tell Chris the truth before it s revealed at his coming-of-age ceremony. With the brotherhood of the Elementals already under strain, will Alex s secret cost them their unity?
