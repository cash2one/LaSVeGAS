1441909:::::The Secret Diary of Adrian Mole Aged 13 3/4:::::Teen angst has never been such serious business - or this much fun! In his secret diary, British teen Adrian Mole excruciatingly details every morsel of his turbulent adolescence. Mixed in with daily reports about the spot sprouting on his chin are heartrending passages about his parents' chaotic marriage. Adrian sees all, and he has something to say about everything. Delightfully self-centered, Adrian is the sort of teen who could rule a much better world - if only his crazy relatives and classmates would get out of his way!
