1706523:::::Book of Margery Kempe:::::"This is an edition of the earliest surviving autobiography of the unique account of the extraordinary life, travels and revelations of Margery Kempe. For the first time the" original text" is presented in an accessible form for modern readers. This is the most compelling and significant English text of the Middle Ages. Provides" on-page annotation," the first of its kind for this book, which brings together the insights of scholarship on Kempe since the discovery of the manuscript in 1934. Also included is a chronology of Kemp's life, a helpful summary analysis of the chapters, and a full bibliography. A must for those interested in the history or literature of the Middle Ages, or for those interested in Women's' history. ALSO AVAILABLE IN HARDCOVER: 0-582-30460-1.
