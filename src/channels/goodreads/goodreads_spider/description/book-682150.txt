682150:::::The Return of Rafe MacKade:::::Ten years after disappearing from Antietam, Maryland, the bad boy has returned. Cleaned up and successful now--and still dangerously good-looking--Rafe MacKade sets the town on fire, and tongues wagging. Lovely newcomer Regan Jones is intrigued--what kind of man could cause this sort of talk? She's just about to find out....
