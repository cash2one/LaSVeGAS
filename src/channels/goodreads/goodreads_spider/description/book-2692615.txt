2692615:::::Cold Town:::::Thirty-seven-year-old Terry Fielding is having a lot of problems the terror of losing his job, a wife who moves further away from him each day, and recurring memories of a little girl named Katie, who died 27 years ago. When he meets Rosina, an enigmatic nightclub hostess, all that stops mattering. Nothing matters but the time they spend together the long nights in cheap hotels where they lie awake and talk about everything. Corruption. Amorality. Escape. But is Rosina really Terry s soulmate? Or is there more to her than meets the eye? By the time Terry find out, it may be too late.
