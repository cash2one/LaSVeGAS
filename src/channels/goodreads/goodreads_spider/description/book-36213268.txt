36213268:::::Love and Werewolves:::::A vampire and a werewolf, both exiles, find love in 1980 America. Alanna, a vampire, has lived on her own in a cabin in the woods for 150 years. She prefers her solitude. One day an injured werewolf, Valko, lands on her doorstep. As she nurses him back to health she finds herself falling in love. But there are forces out there working against them, and secrets that could tear their world apart... *This paranormal romance is part of the Vampire Inheritance Saga universe, though you don't have to have read ant of those to enjoy this as this novel is a prequel.* Love and Werewolves is part of the Venom &amp; Vampires: A Limited Edition Paranormal Romance and Urban Fantasy Collection
