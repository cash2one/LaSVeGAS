3453791:::::Heroes of the Valley:::::Halli Sveinsson has never fit in. Unlike his tall attractive blond siblings, Halli is stumpy, swarthy, with a quick mind inclined to practical jokes. But a trick on Ragnor goes too far, and begins a chain of events that alters his destiny. Leaving home on a hero's quest, he meets highway robbers, terrifying monsters, and a girl who may finally be his match. He discovers the truth about the legends, his family, the meaning of bravery, and himself.
