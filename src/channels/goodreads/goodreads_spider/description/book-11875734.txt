11875734:::::Samana: Luangta Maha Boowa:::::To behold a Samana whose heart is free of defilement that is the highest Blessing. On January 30, 2011 at the age of 97, Ajaan Mah Boowa shed his physical form and totally departed this world. The death of a Buddha or an Arahant disciple is known as Parinibb na. Ajaan Mah Boowa left those who lived and practiced with him in no doubt that he was truly a Noble disciple in the lineage of the Lord Buddha, and a Samana of the highest blessing.
