6079129:::::Waking the Witch:::::From inside the stunning opal necklace, a haunting voice whispered, "Make love to him, Faith. Do it now!" Faith Abbot is a good Puritan woman, but the magic inside an opal necklace begins to torment her with lustful thoughts. For years her heart and body have craved handsome James Parris, a man twelve years her junior, a man she thinks should be courting women his own age. But now the magic of a ghost trapped in a jewel breaks down all her inhibitions. When she and James surrender to the fierce erotic tension that pulses between them, her heart fills with joy. But who is the angry female ghost dwelling within the necklace, wanting revenge?
