17193176:::::Circle In the Sand:::::A collection of slice of life short stories about people going through difficult periods in their life. Stories about coming to terms over a failed summer romance some twenty years after the fact; stories about young people trying to figure out where in society they belong and their struggles between choosing a positive or negative life style; stories about not being able to be with the ones you love, or remain where you feel that you belong, due to circumstances beyond your control; stories where it seems that everything is falling apart, but you hold on anyway in the hopes that it will all work out...
