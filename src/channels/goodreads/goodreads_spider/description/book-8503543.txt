8503543:::::Life Will It Take Me Under!:::::Being the eldest of three children, Kim assumed several responsibilities after her father s death without any complaints. However, problems started to plague the family when Myra entered the picture. The daughter of the family s house tenant, Myra was into drinking and cutting classes. It was not long before the Washington s realized that Myra was a bad influence on Kim. It was through her that Kim became exposed to boys, drugs and sex vices which eventually destroyed her. Kim realized that much of her problems were caused from living at home so she left. How will things turn out for Kim after leaving? Life, Will It Take Me Under is a painful journey of a woman as she searches for the right path and a family s fight to bring back the peace in their home.
