1162836:::::Race/Gender/Media: Considering Diversity Across Audiences, Content, And Producers:::::"Race/Gender/Media" contains 44 different readings that help readers to think critically about issues of race and gender in the media. The readings address a multitude of topics in three major sections: Production, Content, and Audience, and approach the matter of race and gender in the media from rhetorical, social scientific, and critical/cultural perspectives.
