19048521:::::Death Abyss:::::The horrifying new novel, DEATH ABYSS by Korban Blake. Steve Galton is drawn to the seaside resort of Ortown, on the North Norfolk coast - a town that will stop at nothing to retain its sadistic, macabre secrets. When four local boys explore a bricked-up tunnel network, a chain of terrifying events is triggered, all of which lures Galton deeper into a shattering web of unimaginable evil. But first he must decide whom he can trust: the living, or the dead.
