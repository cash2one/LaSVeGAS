14742503:::::Days of Blood and Starlight:::::The thrilling sequel to the extraordinary DAUGHTER OF SMOKE AND BONE . Once upon a time, an angel and a devil fell in love and dared to imagine a new way of living one without massacres and torn throats and bonfires of the fallen, without revenants or bastard armies or children ripped from their mothers arms to take their turn in the killing and dying. Once, the lovers lay entwined in the moon s secret temple and dreamed of a world that was a like a jewel-box without a jewel a paradise waiting for them to find it and fill it with their happiness. This was not that world.
