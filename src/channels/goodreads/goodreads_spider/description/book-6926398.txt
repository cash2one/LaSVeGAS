6926398:::::My Husband's Woman:::::Young and beautiful twenty-year-old Meshia can have any man she wants, but who she can't get enough of is her thirty-eight-year-old married lover, Byron. Successful attorney Byron enjoys being the world of two women:a wife he loves and a mistress he lust for. Wife Alexandra has based her sanity on the faithfulness of her husband Byron. When she discovers their hidden affair, she takes Byron and Meshia into a bizarre world, one of which they could never have dreamed of, not even in their worst of nightmares.
