25615392:::::A Sister's Promise:::::Two sisters. Bound by blood. Torn apart by love. My sister the glue that held our family together and the gatekeeper to the memories of our shared childhood. The girl I made a pact with to protect each other for life. The woman who destroyed my family, my future. And the only one who can save my daughter. Set against the dramatic backdrop of India, A Sister s Promise is a powerful, emotional tale of family secrets, love and the ties that bind sisters together.
