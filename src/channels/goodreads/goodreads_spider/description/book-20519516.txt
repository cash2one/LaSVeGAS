20519516:::::Escaping Fate:::::Escaping Fate is the story of two lovers, each seeking to escape and forget their past. Set beneath the blazing sun in the British India of the late 19th century, it follows the passionate romance and adventures of American Jack Wilde, serving in the British cavalry, and Kitty O'Keefe, a free-thinking newspaper woman as they seek to overcome countless obstacles and find lasting peace in each other's embrace.
