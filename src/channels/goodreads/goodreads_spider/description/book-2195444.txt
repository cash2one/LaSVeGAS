2195444:::::Caroline's Mystery Dolls:::::Caroline Pearce is always bragging about her collection of porcelain dolls, but no one has ever seen them. Some of her friends, especially Jessica Wakefield, wonder if Caroline really has dolls at all. When Caroline opens her case to take out the doll she brought to class for show-and-tell, there's nothing inside! Did someone take the doll, or was Caroline lying all along?
