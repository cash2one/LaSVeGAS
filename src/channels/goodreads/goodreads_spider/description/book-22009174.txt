22009174:::::Flight:::::Flight, The Last Paladin Series is an Urban Fantasy story set in two worlds, present day Florida and an alternate world named Irlendria. Sentenced to death for his Bloodright, Start m Ironwolf has fought for life since the time of his birth. Born a half-breed and a Paladin of Ukko, he is the first of his kind. When his Father s people, the Klavikians, are destroyed in an overwhelming surprise attack by invading Toenellian Demonic Hordes, Start m is spirited away to Earth by his Grandsire, Leader of the Shadowfang Pack. Hiding among Humans, Start m lives in the shadows as he grows and learns to control his powers. On the eve of his eighteenth birthday, the fates of both worlds become irrevocably entwined when the Tuonellians discover Start m on Earth. Suddenly, Mankind is swept into the middle of a millennium long conflict spanning two worlds and Start m must find a way to fight his people s ancient enemy. If not, Humanity will suffer the same fate as the Klavikians.
