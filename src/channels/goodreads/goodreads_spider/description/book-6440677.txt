6440677:::::Pandora Hearts, Volume 8:::::The snow-white maiden encountered once upon a time in the depths of the Abyss by the clown christened the Red-Eyed Specter who was she really? As there are two sides to every story, could it be there are two sides to the existence known as Alice? Duke Barma may have provided a sordid outline, but Break colors the tale with vivid swathes of bloody crimson as he opens up about his past. Will these tragic truths long secreted away isolate Break further from his allies once he has revealed them?
