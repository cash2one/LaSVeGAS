109242:::::Swamp Thing, Vol. 5: Earth to Earth:::::Written by Alan Moore; Art by John Totleben, Rick Veitch, and Alfredo Alcala Returned from his sojourn to hell, Swamp Thing discovers that his girlfriend Abby is being persecuted for their unnatural relations.
