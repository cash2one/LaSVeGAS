After all, if a man good as his daddy couldn t do it right, what chance does Jackson have? One Undeniable Attraction These two vulnerable souls are as different as cornbread and ketchup, but they fit together like sweet butter on hot biscuits. Short-term, they re exactly what the other needs. But when their hearts get involved, they re both gonna end up with a big ol case of Southern Fried Blues.
