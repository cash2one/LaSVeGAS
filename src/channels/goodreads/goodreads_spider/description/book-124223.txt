124223:::::Sir David: The Life and Loves of a Welsh Knight:::::Sir David is the true story of a giant Welsh warrior knight torn between his devotion to Wales and the English House of York during the Wars of the Roses in the 15th century. Love, honor and friendship issues plus the conflicts of David and wife Wellian who have twelve children plus an illegitimate child are sensitive and real. Sir David provides, in researched detail, a clear view of old England, its politics, man-to-man battles and manor life. This compelling novel depicts themes of home, family life, war, friendship, loneliness and love that span the centuries.
