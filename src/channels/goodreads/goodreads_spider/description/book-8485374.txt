8485374:::::Digitalis:::::Step into the boots of a former Marine in this heart-pounding adventure in life and love. Colton Cowboy Neeley is a Marine trying to find his footing as he battles flashbacks now that he s back home. Piper Blum is a woman in hiding from life and the assassins bent on destroying her family. When their hearts collide, more than their lives are at stake. Will Colton find a way to forgive Piper s lies? Can Piper find a way to rescue her father, trapped in Israel? Is there any way their love, founded on her lies, can survive?
