76826:::::Exile and the Kingdom:::::These six stories, written at the height of Camus' artistic powers, all depict people at decisive, revelatory moments in their lives. Translated by Justin O'Brien. The six works collected in this volume are: "The Adulterous Woman" ("La Femme adult re") "The Renegade or a Confused Spirit" ("Le Ren gat ou un esprit confus") "The Silent Men" ("Les Muets") "The Guest" ("L'H te") "Jonas or the Artist at Work" ("Jonas ou l artiste au travail") "The Growing Stone" ("La Pierre qui pousse")
