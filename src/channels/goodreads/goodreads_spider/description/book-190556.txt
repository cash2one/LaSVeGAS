190556:::::The Heavens Declare: Astrological Ages and the Evolution of Consciousness:::::In The Heavens Declare, Alice O. Howell explains, We are not confronting the end of the world, but the end of the Age of Pisces! Integrating two major disciplines, she reveals the fascinating connection between astrology and Jung's Theory of the Collective Unconscious unfolding through 10,000 years of history in the sequence of previous astronomical Ages.The proof lies in the undeniable synchronicity of religons, and mythologies, supported by the archaeology of each eon. The book also prepares the reader seeking to understand the signs, houses, and aspects.
