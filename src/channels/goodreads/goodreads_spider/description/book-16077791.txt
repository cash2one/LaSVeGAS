16077791:::::Imagined Love:::::Jade Caldwell is a hopeless romantic who, despite all of the pain she s witnessed and ultimately experiences, still believes in love. A coming of age story set in Gary, Indiana, Imagined Love spans the life, love, and heartache of Jade and those closest to her. As unexpected as life, Imagined Love, offers twist and turns that will cause laughter, tears, and the joy which comes after the storm.
