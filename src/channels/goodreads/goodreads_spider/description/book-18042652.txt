18042652:::::Revelation:::::Book 3 in the Forever Series. Liv is gaining control of her true powers, a change that begins to alter her sense of identity. Revelations she has pushed for, come to pass, but Liv realizes that learning of her past has only made it catch up with her. Old enemies and new friends all make for an exciting new chapter in Liv s life but does she have the strength to see it through?
