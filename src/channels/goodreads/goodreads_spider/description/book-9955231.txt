9955231:::::The Making of Rapper Dirty Ryse:::::A robbery in broad daylight a vengeful ex-murderer a talented rappers life is in danger and all that happened because of need or greed? You decide. Rapper (Daryle) Dirty Ryse and his cohort (Raymond) Clean Washington would never be able to take back the outcome of what they did the night they robbed a helpless senior citizen on an Oakland, California AC Transit bus. After lives were lost and dreams were routed -Ryse lived out the brutal conse-quences of his actions wishing that he could take back what happened on that life-changing summer eve of 2006. As he and Clean lived in the moment; they had no idea to what degree one horrible slip-up would affect so many lives. How can their lives be saved?
