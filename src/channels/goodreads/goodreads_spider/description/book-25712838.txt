25712838:::::A Sweet Surrender:::::New York, 1777 Siaragowaeh of the Onyota aka tribe has a love for all things living and when a fallen British soldier becomes severely wounded, Siara secretly nurtures the officer back to health, putting her own position in her tribe at risk. Sergeant James Blake of His Majesty s elite assault troops vaguely remembers the ambush that left him at the mercy of a beautiful native woman a woman he isn t yet certain he can trust. When a narrow escape from Siara s village forces them on the trail together, desire soon flares even brighter between them. Yet in a journey driven by duty and complicated by loyalty, Siara and James must determine if they will follow their predestined paths or surrender to their hearts and chart anew
