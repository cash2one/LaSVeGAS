6261122:::::Killer Pizza:::::Pizza you ll die for! Toby McGill dreams of becoming a world-famous chef, but up until now, his only experience has been watching the Food Network. When Toby lands a summer job at Killer Pizza, where pies like The Monstrosity and The Frankensausage are on the menu, things seem perfect. His coworkers, Annabel and Strobe, are cool, and Toby loves being part of a team. But none of them are prepared for what s really going on at Killer Pizza: It s a front for a monster-hunting organization! Learning to cook pizzas is one thing, but killing hideously terrifying monsters? That s a whole other story. Still, if Toby quits Killer Pizza, will monsters take over his town?
