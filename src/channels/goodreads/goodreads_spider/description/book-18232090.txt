18232090:::::Blues In The Key Of B:::::Blues In The Key Of B is the third installment in the ongoing story of a blues singer for whom the blues is all too real. In this offering, new mother Bobbie Brooks faces unforeseen troubles as she continues the fight to keep her life on track and hold her family together. Meanwhile, her niece, Tomeka, has a story of her own to tell. Fans of Bluesday and Lovely Blues, get ready for Blues In The Key Of B!
