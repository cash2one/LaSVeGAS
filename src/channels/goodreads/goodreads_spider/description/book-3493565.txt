3493565:::::Qaisar o Kisra /:::::Qaiser-o-Kisra (English: Caesar and Khosrow) is set in the backdrop of Byzantine Sasanian War of 602 628 and the Rise of Islam in Arabian Peninsula. It follows Asim, a young Arab, who leaves his homeland to escape the barbarism there in search for peace and finds himself caught between a bloody war between two great powers of the world.
