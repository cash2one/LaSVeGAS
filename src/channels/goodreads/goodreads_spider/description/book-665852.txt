665852:::::Man's Search for Himself:::::"Analyzes life as we are living it, &amp; the analysis is truthful &amp; profound."--New York Times Loneliness, boredom, emptiness: These are the complaints that Rollo May encountered over &amp; over from his patients. In response, he probes the hidden layers of personality to reveal the core of human integration--a basic &amp; inborn sense of value. Man's Search for Himself is an illuminating view of our predicament in an age of overwhelming anxieties &amp; gives guidance on how to choose, judge &amp; act during suchtimes.
