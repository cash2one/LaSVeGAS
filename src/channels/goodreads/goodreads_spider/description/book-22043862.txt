22043862:::::Dark Angel:::::Having narrowly averted the Apocalypse, Porsche has a new position as a Guardian Angel on Earth. She s looking forward to Valentine s Day when her boyfriend Alex decides he wants to break up. While at the Purgatory Bar, pondering the many changes in her life, Porsche meets a new man. Devlin seems perfect in every way. Could he be the perfect rebound fling or is he maybe just a little too perfect?
