2420801:::::Beyond the Dark Bayou:::::"BEYOND THE DARK BAYOU -- A True Story of Survival through Spritual Inspiration" is the heartbreaking yet inspirational true story of John and Alice Ball and their family, set in the Louisiana lands of magnolias, weeping willows, and Spanish moss. Beyond The Dark Bayou retells the almost Biblical tale of how a young family with a beautiful, promising life ahead of them suddenly faced catastrophic hardships and an endless chain of medical nightmares, but persevered throughout it all, guided by spiritual inspiration. ." . . .reads like Steel Magnolias meets Erin Brokaich"
