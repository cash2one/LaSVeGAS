200835:::::Sitt Marie Rose:::::Translated from the French by Georgina Kleege, Sitt Marie Rose , is the story of a woman abducted by militiamen during the Civil War in Lebanon. It reveals the tribal mentality which makes the Middle East a dangerous powerhouse. It constitutes a new narrative form and is already a classic of war literature. It won the France-Pays Arabes award in Paris and has been translated into six languages.
