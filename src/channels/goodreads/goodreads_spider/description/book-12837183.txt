12837183:::::The Blood:::::The riveting conclusion to a thrilling trilogy from New York Times bestselling master of suspense D. J. MacHale. From Marshall s mysterious experiences in The Light to Cooper s haunting perspective in The Black, the questions have intensified along with the action. And now it is time for answers: A final showdown is in order between best friends Marshall and Cooper and the terrifying villain Damon, who s more determined than ever to break down the walls between the worlds of the living and the dead. Marshall is forced to make a brave and shocking choice when the battle is on the line, and he and Cooper might be rewarded with help from someone quite unexpected . As the gripping trilogy heads toward resolution, shocks and surprises await at the end of the long and winding Morpheous Road.
