119390:::::If You Meet the Buddha on the Road, Kill Him: The Pilgrimage Of Psychotherapy Patients:::::Therapists do not and cannot give answers. Explore the true nature of the therapeutic relationship, and realize that the guru is no Buddha. He is just another human struggling. Understanding the shape of your own personal ills will lead you on your journey to recovery.
