933863:::::On the Couch:::::Lorraine Bracco is known to millions as psychiatrist Dr. Melfi on HBO's The Sopranos . It's hard to imagine that this formidable woman spent years struggling to free herself from depression, serious money problems, and a disastrous relationship that led to a widely-publicized child-custody battle. Here, she openly reveals the details of her struggle-and the treatment that helped her triumph.
