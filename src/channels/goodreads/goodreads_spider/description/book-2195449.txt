2195449:::::The Twins' Big Pow-Wow:::::Family Pride When Jessica and Elizabeth Wakefield spend Thanksgiving weekend on a Mohave reservation, they discover that not all Native Americans wear headdresses, sleep in tepees, and do rain dances. Water Lily, or Ann, as she likes to be called, certainly doesn't. She hates everything to do with Indian traditions especially her grandfather, Gray Eagle. Can the twins make Ann proud of her roots?
