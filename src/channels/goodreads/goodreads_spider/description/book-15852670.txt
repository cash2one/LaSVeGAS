15852670:::::Ode to Uke:::::Ode to Uke by Arthur Ravenscrag III, Paul Andrews (Illustrator). Ode to Uke is love story like no other. A clever and humorous dedication to the ukulele, you ll laugh as you strum along. Including illustrations, songs to learn - Ode to Uke takes a look at the ukulele in a way you ve never seen it before. Chapters devoted to metal, country, old Hollywood, Hawaii and the heroes of the ukulele, this little instrument will steal your heart. It doesn t matter if you re an expert or you ve just been given your first ukulele, Ode to Uke is for everyone.
