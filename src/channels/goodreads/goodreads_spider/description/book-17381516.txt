17381516:::::Healing Grace:::::Can Abigail overcome the pain caused by her abusive father and find love and acceptance in this Amish community? SERIES DESCRIPTION The Touch of Grace trilogy series is about three faithful women who, when faced with three separate hardships, are torn between the men they love and staying true to themselves.Grace saves. Even in a world where retribution is almost expected, the Amish demonstrate with beautiful mercy that all can be pardoned, showing the deep-rooted forgiveness that dwells within them.
