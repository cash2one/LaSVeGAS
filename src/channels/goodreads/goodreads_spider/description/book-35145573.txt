35145573:::::Love Connection:::::Have you ever wondered what might have been? Gemma Dawson is at the airport, staring at two plane tickets to two different cities. Two different weddings. Two possible futures. She s at a crossroads. Be maid of honor at her best friend s wedding or crash her ex s? Gemma s decision, unknown to her, hinges on a delayed flight and a chance meeting. Now her life is about to go down two parallel tracks will Gemma fly toward a life with her first love or a future with a man she s not even met yet? Love Connection is a feel good Romantic Comedy about one woman, life s infinite possibilities, and the destiny that lies beyond two different choices.
