25945080:::::Z Plan: Homecoming:::::Cale's unexpected journey began while deployed to Iraq as a U.S. Army soldier in Z Plan: Blood on the Sand. When a ferocious virus spread across the globe, his military training proved not to be enough. In Z Plan: Red Tides he traveled on land, sea and air as he desperately tried to reach his family back home. Now on U.S. soil, Cale's limits are put to the final test as he pushes his way through the undead. But something even more sinister follows his every move. Z Plan: Homecoming is the thrilling conclusion of the Z Plan Trilogy.
