37643484:::::The Unexpected Warrior : an Ace Stone adventure:::::Growing up in 1960 s Britain, desperate to be a success and break away from his humble beginnings, budding entrepreneur Ace Stone is young, charming and cash rich from his illegal boxing promotions and his uncanny knack for creative business dealings that land him the dream life he always wanted. However, there s a cost for his success as the veiled lady of his subconscious haunts and taunts him, Ace s ventures take an even darker turn that includes money-laundering, drugs, betrayal and murder. His loyalty is tested between the two rival Mob families in the middle of a bloody war and Ace has to pull off the biggest deal of his life to save himself and the life he has so desperately fought for, but at what at cost?
