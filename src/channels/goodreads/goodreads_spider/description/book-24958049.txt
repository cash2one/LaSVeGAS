24958049:::::Married To A Deadly Bitch 2:::::Picking up right where Married To A Bitch left off, Married to a Vindictive Bitch is definitely action packed and drama filled. At the end of the last installment, Andre has a bomb dropped on him when he finds out that Myia may carry a seed in the womb that belongs to him. This news is followed by the news that Symone has a seed in the womb. Andre faces all of this drama in the midst of possibly losing a friendship and partnership that has lasted over twenty years. Will Andre be able to find a way to clean up his mess or will it consume him as he deals with being married to a bitch now vindictive.
