36860591:::::The Write Prescription: Finding the "Right" Spiritual Dosage to Overcome Any Obstacle:::::A self-help, inspirational/motivational book that uses the personal journal of Dr. Amber Robins to describe the challenges she went through in medical school and how her faith helped her overcome her obstacles. The author uses various Scripture verses that are inspiring to any reader trying to overcome any challenge and provide insightful prayers that will encourage self-reflection and perseverance in life.
