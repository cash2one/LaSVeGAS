2914005:::::The Fat Innkeeper:::::Hotel detective Am Caulfield's life is more hectic than usual when the doctor investigating fraud at the Union of Near-Death Experiences Retreat (UNDER) is found dead. With the new owner, Hiroshi Yamada (aka The Fat Innkeeper), looking over his shoulder, and hormone-crazed swingers running amok, Am investigates the UNDER practitioners as they commune with the dead--and finds himself on the trail of a very clever murderer.
