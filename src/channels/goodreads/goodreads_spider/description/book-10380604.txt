10380604:::::The Devil; What does he look like:::::He is one the most terrifying figure in all of literature and art. Every culture has a manifestation of him. He is the essence of our nightmares. Satan, although called by many names, has the same persona around the world evil. With an unbiased eye, Nwaocha Ogechukwu explores the many manifestations of the devil in religion and the consequence effects on culture and the arts starting from days of ancient pagans to contemporary artisans. This academic work is an enlightening experience that should not be missed.
