14476866:::::Daffodil: The Clumsy Fairy:::::Daffodil lives in Dixieland. A bustling magical city inside a giant oak tree. However she is cursed. Cursed to live a life as an accident prone. Her friends do not wish to be around her long enough to have a conversation and she finds it hard to help out in Dixieland as everyone fears her and her unnatural trait. However, when two Ravens come to destroy the peace in Dixieland is this Daffodil's time to shine?
