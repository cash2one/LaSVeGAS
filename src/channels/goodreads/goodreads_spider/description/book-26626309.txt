26626309:::::How Many Friends Could a Bibbolybob Make If a Bibbolybob Came to Earth?: Aka Wibbolywub and the Earthlings:::::A happy, quirky, colourful story about an alien visitor to Earth who makes friends with lots of different Earthlings and finds out all about them. Ideal for reading to young children to introduce them to numbers and counting. The digits zero to ten are incorporated into the story so that children can familiarize themselves with what they look like and realize that numbers are everywhere and nothing to worry about.
