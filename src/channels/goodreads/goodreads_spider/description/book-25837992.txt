25837992:::::My Father's Son:::::When Nathan s father, a decorated Navy SEAL, is killed in combat, he must rely on his father s teammates for direction as he learns to become a man. The normal struggles of adolescence are amplified while growing up in the shadow of a war hero, and a young man s future hangs in the balance. No one is safe from the scars of war in this funny, heart-wrenching, poignant novel.
