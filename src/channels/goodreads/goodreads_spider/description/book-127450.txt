127450:::::As Long as We Both Shall Live:::::A change is coming, April Lancaster s fortune cookie reads. Be prepared. But how could she be prepared for the news that she has an inoperable brain tumor? April s life will never be the same. Then she meets handsome Mark Gianni. Mark has cystic fibrosis, but he also has a passion for life . . . and for April. When he asks April to marry him, she s happier than she s ever been. April thought she and Mark would be together forever. But since Mark s death, April has never felt more alone. Then Brandon Benedict comes into her life. Brandon is lonely and angry he and April have a lot in common. But April cannot tell Brandon about her illness. When April s medical problems suddenly return, she must decide what to tell Brandon. Can the love she s felt before help her now?
