22540002:::::One Last Time:::::Grief counselor and skeptic Claire Devane finds her life turned upside down when the recently deceased begin to return from the hereafter to visit the living one last time. Secrets are revealed. Love and longing... betrayal, murder, guilt... the visitations bring them all out of the shadows and into the light. It's a strange new world, one that threatens to swallow her whole. Full-length novel, little profanity, no explicit sex, mild violence.
