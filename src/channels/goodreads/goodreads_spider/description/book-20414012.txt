20414012:::::Valentine Blunders:::::Ava Mathias, young, Amish, and beautiful and newly married to Ezekiel (Zeke) Mathias finds herself in several dilemmas. First she must prove to the church elder, which happens to be Zeke s uncle, that she is still Amish at heart. Then she is at loss as to what to do to celebrate her fourth-month wedding anniversary. John Troyer, the youngest of Ava s three older brothers, takes Zeke s cousin, Helen, out for dinner where then Helen asks the wrong question. Ava s wayward brother, Abe, finds himself in serious trouble with his on-again, off-again girlfriend, Bliss. Isaac Troyer, Ava s oldest brother has had enough and leaves town while their Aunt Rachael sees a ghost from her past.
