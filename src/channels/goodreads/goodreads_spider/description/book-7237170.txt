7237170:::::In the Darkest Night:::::Fleeing from both her dark heritage and the magical council she attempted to steal from, Farran s greatest fear is to be sent back to the father she has utterly betrayed. Yet when a demon attempts to capture her, Farran knows she cannot stay hidden. She must find help. Kel Andrews is a magical troubleshooter with troubles of his own. Recovering from being kidnapped and tortured by darksiders, Kel has been removed from active duty by the magical council. When the mysterious Farran collapses on his doorstep, begging for help, Kel feels compelled to assist her in any way possible. As danger both demonic and human closes in on them from every side, Kel and Farran must learn to trust each other as they battle the monsters that are determined to keep them apart.
