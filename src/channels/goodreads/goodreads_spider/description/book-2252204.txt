2252204:::::Tiny Deaths:::::Analyzing the complexity, absurdity, and blessedness of seemingly ordinary people, this debut collection examines the metaphysical assumptions surrounding death. From the end of a relationship to the meaning behind its title, this anthology continually surprises and subverts, utilizing topics such as alien intelligence, reincarnation, imaginary children, and even conversations with Hitler s childhood pet. Engaging and diverse, this compendium offers a fascinating perspective on mortality.
