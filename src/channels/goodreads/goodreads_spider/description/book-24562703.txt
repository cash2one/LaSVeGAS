24562703:::::The Boulders of My Life that shaped my Journey:::::True story about recovering from Post Traumatic Stress Disorder. The author tells what happened to her as a child, teenager, adult and what happened to her in the military. This is a story about courage, not giving up, and healing from the unspeakable. She includes her personal journal that details what was going on at the time and what she was feeling. She explains what happens to a person when he or she is raped. How there lives are changed forever and tells how her human rights were not respected. She wrote this to educate the public and to help other survivors heal from being raped. She wants to be a voice to make change happen. This is a must read.
