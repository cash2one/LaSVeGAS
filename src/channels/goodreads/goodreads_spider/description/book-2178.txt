2178:::::Flaubert in Egypt: A Sensibility on Tour:::::At once a classic of travel literature and a penetrating portrait of a sensibility on tour, Flaubert in Egypt wonderfully captures the young writer s impressions during his 1849 voyages. Using diaries, letters, travel notes, and the evidence of Flaubert s traveling companion, Maxime Du Camp, Francis Steegmuller reconstructs his journey through the bazaars and brothels of Cairo and down the Nile to the Red Sea.
