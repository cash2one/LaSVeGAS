9709305:::::From Pack to Pride:::::Book one in the Supernatural Mates Series When a wolf shifter joins a pride of lions, the fur flies. When Adrian is sent to live with the lion pride for his own safety, little does he know that the pride alpha is already culling him from the pack. With one picture, Talan knew Adrian was his mate. Now he has six months to persuade Adrian to stay in his pride forever and keep a psychotic wolf from killing him.
