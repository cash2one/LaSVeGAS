21805717:::::Only Ever Yours:::::In a world in which baby girls are no longer born naturally, women are bred in schools, trained in the arts of pleasing men until they are ready for the outside world. At graduation, the most highly rated girls become companions , permitted to live with their husbands and breed sons until they are no longer useful. For the girls left behind, the future as a concubine or a teacher is grim. Best friends Freida and Isabel are sure they ll be chosen as companions they are among the most highly rated girls in their year. But as the intensity of final year takes hold, Isabel does the unthinkable and starts to put on weight. .. And then, into this sealed female environment, the boys arrive, eager to choose a bride. Freida must fight for her future even if it means betraying the only friend, the only love, she has ever known. . .
