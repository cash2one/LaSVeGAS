17259389:::::The Stone Seekers:::::In 1000 B.C., two 10-year-old boys, Zeth and Chase, go on a quest to save a dragon that has been entombed in stone. They must find seven magical stones guarded by seven evil beings in order to get the stones. With the help of Xander, the First-warrior in arms, Gideon the elf, and a magical sword, they undergo a dangerous journey filled with trials. If they succeed, they will have a treasure they never thought existed.
