21685602:::::From the Heart, Volume 1:::::An inspirational collection of essays, poems and thoughts on nurturing our inner child. It offers a little something for everyone, from a good mental cup of tea, to discussing the importance of play, to a serene sailboat with orcas. Crystal Linn is a freelance writer and award winning poet who also teaches creative writing classes for adults and children, and does public speaking. She lives in the Seattle area with her husband, where she enjoys reading mysteries, writing poetry and sailing with family and friends.
