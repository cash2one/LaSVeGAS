3284192:::::Zorgamazoo:::::Zorgamazoo is the story of a young girl; a shadowy, subterranean creature; a bizarre lottery; a secret map; an imminent alien invasion; and a sport that brings together the finer points of cricket, swimming and chess. It also has lobotomies. In summation, it is about saving the universe from boredom. And it rhymes all 281 pages worth.
