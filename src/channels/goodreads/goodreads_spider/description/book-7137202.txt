7137202:::::Sidney Sheldon's After the Darkness:::::Author Tillie Bagshawe brilliantly recaptured the late superstar author s magic with Sidney Sheldon s Mistress of the Game and now she does it again with Sidney Sheldon s After the Darkness. A classic tale of love and betrayal, and a struggle for survival in the new world order, this is an enthralling novel with ripped-from-the-headlines immediacy, perfect for the post-Bernie Madoff era in America. A tribute to one of America s most popular and bestselling authors, Sidney Sheldon s After the Darkness is a novel that the master himself would have been proud to call his own.
