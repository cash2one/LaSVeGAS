31926533:::::Penelope Gilbert and the Children of Azure:::::When 13 year-old Penelope Gilbert accidentally transforms into a stapler during math class, she s sure she's going crazy. But she s not imagining the men in black suits now patrolling the halls at school, nor is she imagining the new substitute teacher who orders the class to take a special new test. A test that requires blood. Hunted for her powers, and torn from the life she knows, Penny is swept up into a world in the clouds where magic meets machine and pirates rule the sky.
