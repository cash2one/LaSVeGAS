285740:::::Stephanie's Ponytail:::::None of the kids in her class wear a ponytail, so Stephanie decides she must have one. The loud, unanimous comment from her classmates is: Ugly, ugly, very ugly. Steadfast, when all the girls have copied her ponytail, she resolves to try a new style. With true Munsch flair, each of Stephanie s ponytails is more outrageous than the last, while the cast of copycats grows and grows.
