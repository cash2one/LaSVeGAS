5318660:::::Rogues & Heroes of the Island of Newfoundland:::::Rogues and Heroes tells the stories of fascinating Newfoundlanders, with an emphasis on the pre-1949 era. Some, such as opera star Georgina Stirling, were adored; others, like Peter Easton, were feared; still more inhabited that misty landscape that makes them rogues to some and heroes to others. Open the book for a compelling journey into the island's colourful past.
