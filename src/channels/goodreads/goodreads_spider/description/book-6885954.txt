6885954:::::Sojourner:::::Seventeen-year-old Elizabeth Moon has been dreaming of her murder her entire life, and in those dreams, a dark presence is there, watching. When she returns home to Hauser s Landing, the very place her father disappeared, she comes in contact with a gorgeous boy named Lev Walker, and it s not long before she s falling in love. But there s something wrong with Lev. When she realizes he s the eerie watcher in her dreams, she ll have to discover the truth. Is he a guardian angel or a sojourner, an angel of death who has come to collect her soul?
