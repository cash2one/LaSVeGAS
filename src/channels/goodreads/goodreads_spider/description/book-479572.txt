479572:::::Anarchy, State, and Utopia:::::In this brilliant and widely acclaimed book, Robert Nozick challenges the most commonly held political and social positions of our age liberal, socialist, and conservative. It won the 1975 U.S. National Book Award in category Philosophy and Religion, has been translated into 11 languages, and was named one of the "100 most influential books since the war" (1945 1995) by the U.K. Times Literary Supplement.
