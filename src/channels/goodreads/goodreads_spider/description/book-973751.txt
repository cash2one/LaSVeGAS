973751:::::Jack Smith: Flaming Creature : His Amazing Life and Times:::::Flaming Creature, the catalog to accompany the multimedia retrospective of one of the late 20th century's most important experimental filmmakers, is a hodge-podge encyclopedia of his life. Including almost every piece of ephemera from performances, with essays by Larry Rinder, Ronald Tavel, Nayland Blake, J. Hoberman, and Penny Arcade.
