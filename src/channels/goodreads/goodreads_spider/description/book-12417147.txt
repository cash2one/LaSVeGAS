12417147:::::Time's Secret:::::What would you do if you were on a quest you didn't like to find an object you didn't want... If there was a prophecy that hinted you could not succeed... If an oracle warned that everyone close to you would perish if you failed... If those closest to you were keeping secrets that affected not only your quest but your very life... If the truth about your life and destiny was the most closely guarded secret of all... Most secrets are revealed by Time... But what do you do when time is running out? Time's Secret is the second book in the Time's Edge sci-fi/fantasy series.
