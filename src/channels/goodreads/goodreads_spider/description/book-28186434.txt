28186434:::::Finn Family Moomintroll:::::It is spring in the valley and the Moomins are ready for adventure! Moomintroll and his friends Snufkin and Sniff find the Hobgoblin's top hat, all shiny and new and just waiting to be taken home. They soon realize that this is no ordinary hat; it can turn anything--or anyone--into something else! (Note the translator is Elizabeth Portch. Ernest Benn Ltd is the translator's employer.)
