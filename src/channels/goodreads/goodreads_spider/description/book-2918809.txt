2918809:::::The House: The History of the House of Representatives:::::Throughout America's history, the House has played a central role in shaping the nation's destiny. In this incomparable single-volume history, distinguished historian Robert V. Remini traces the institution from a struggling, nascent body to the venerable powerhouse it has become since America's rise on the world stage. The essential drama of democracy the struggle between principle and pragmatism is showcased throughout the book, and through it the history of America's successful experiment with democracy unfurls.
