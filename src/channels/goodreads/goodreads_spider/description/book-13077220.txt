13077220:::::Speculative Journeys: Scifi, Fantasy and Other Strange Tales:::::Explore the strange and endless possibilities of the future. How desperate will humans become? Where will exploration, science and politics lead us? Regardless of where you go to seek answers--a distant moon, the back streets of a dystopian society, a grave--the stories within these pages will keep you wandering. Speculative Journeys is an anthology of T. M. Crone's previously published short stories.
