6675124:::::Shattered Images:::::Born to a strong-willed and cruel mother with whom she would be in constant conflict and a passive father whom she adored, Jennifer Vicci spent much of her childhood away in a convert school, returning home as a teenager. Wavering between fear and rebellion at her mother's rigid standards, Jennifer secretly meets her first love, a handsome marine. But fate steps in to part them, and she is propelled into a series of devastating marriages.
