317500:::::The Lion, the Witch and the Wardrobe:::::The Lion, the Witch &amp; the Wardrobe, completed in the winter of 1949 &amp; published in 1950, tells the story of four ordinary children: Peter, Susan, Edmund &amp; Lucy Pevensie. They discover a wardrobe in Prof. Digory Kirke's house that leads to the magical land of Narnia, which is currently under the spell of a witch. The four children fulfill an ancient, mysterious prophecy while in Narnia. The Pevensie children help Aslan (the Turkish word for lion) &amp; his army save Narnia from the evil White Witch, who's reigned over the Narnia in winter for 100 years. This edition follows the original numbering scheme. Recent publishers have renumbered the volumes so they are ordered chronologically. This was reportedly the author's preference. Other editions number this book as 2.
