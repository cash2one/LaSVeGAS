35545:::::The Complete Beatles Recording Sessions: The Official Story of the Abbey Road Years 1962-1970:::::This is EMI Records' official diary-format history of every Beatles recording session. Over 350 color black &amp; white photographs and illustrations, including rare photos by Linda McCartney and the first facsimile reproductions of Abbey Road recording sheets, tape boxes, album sleeve roughs, memos, contracts, press releases and much more.
