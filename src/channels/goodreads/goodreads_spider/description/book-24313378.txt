24313378:::::Quest and Crew:::::Quest and Crew is a classic adventure story set in paradise aboard one of Canada s best built ocean cruising sailboats. The book is the true account of how a husband and wife become captivated by the dream of sailing the blue waters of the Caribbean. Quest and Crew is all about overcoming challenges and following a dream. It is a humorous and inspiring account of transforming a beautiful classic sailing yacht and her rookie crew into a well seasoned team prepared to meet the dangers of the open ocean. The story opens with a major hurricane approaching the south coast of Grenada. David, Wendy and Quest survive the fiercest storm in the Caribbean with barely a scratch. The narrative then circles back to when David first discovered the crazy idea of sailing to paradise. Join David and Wendy as they sail into the sunset.
