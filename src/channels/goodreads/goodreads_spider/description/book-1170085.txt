1170085:::::Incidents of Travel in Yucatan, Vol 1:::::Volume 1 of 2-volume set. Classic (1843) exploration of jungles of Yucatan, looking for evidences of Maya civilization. Extensive accounts of 44 Maya sites as well as of Yucatan folkways, manners, dress, ceremonies, amusements all of which makes this a great travel book. Total in set: 127 engravings. 1 map.
