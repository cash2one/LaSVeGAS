574131:::::The Enchanted Barn:::::Condemned! Their old, crumbling apartment building had been condemned and would soon be demolished. Shirley Hollister had four weeks to find a place for her mother and little brothers and sisters to live. At last she found a beautiful old stone barn in the country and courageously asked the owner, young Sidney Graham, if she could rent it for the summer. Though he was amazed by her request, he agreed and became close friends with the family as the months passed. Then, one day, Shirley didn't come home from work. . . .
