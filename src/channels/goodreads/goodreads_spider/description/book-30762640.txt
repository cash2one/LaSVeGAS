30762640:::::Bigger the Secrets: Greater the Passion:::::A brainy and professionally savvy San Diego girl falls hard for a tall handsome and ridiculously rich business man whose destiny consists of becoming the sole heir to the throne of his family owned high-end silk, international clothing brand. She is a sexologist at the top of her game and he is a pro athlete. It so happens, the two of them match together like oil and water, yet they can t walk away. Ironically, murder provides the lithification which binds them together like bedrock. They are propelled predominantly by deep secrets, great passion, and of course, a murdering priest--what else?
