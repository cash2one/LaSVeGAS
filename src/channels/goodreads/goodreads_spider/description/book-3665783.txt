3665783:::::Saga of the Wolf:::::NICOLAS: BOOK 1 Nicolas wants to be mortal again, and Adam Lang, his hated enemy is the key to that mortality. As the alpha male, his desire to rid himself of what he sees as a curse doesn't sit well with the pack. Soon, he and Adam, will be on the run from werewolves, and headed straight for a demon.and all the time, they could be falling in love.
