17085869:::::Lifting Up Our Hearts - 150 Selected Prayers from John Calvin:::::"In this book you will find 150 of Calvin's prayers, arranged by subject, along with the Scripture passage on which Calvin was lecturing. Each prayer relates to the theme of the biblical text Calvin was expounding, which is why the Scripture text has been included. With the Spirit's blessing, the prayers will awaken your affections for the glory of God and point you to Christ, the one of whom all the Scriptures speak. Prayers are arranged thematically in sections, and each section begins with a quotation from the "Institutes of the Christian Religion." ~from the introduction
