7043779:::::Die Megabit Bombe:::::Bomba Magabitowa is the second work (after Tajemnica Chinskiego Pokoju) that continues the famous and classic essay from Lem's Summa Technologiae. Summa was first published in 1964, Tajemnica - four years ago - as a collection of essays published in PC Magazine. Bomba Megabitowa is the continuation and crowning of this collection. Professor Jerzy Jarzebski Bomba Magabitowa was not translated into English.
