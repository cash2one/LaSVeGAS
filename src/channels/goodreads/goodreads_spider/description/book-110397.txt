110397:::::How to Marry a Marquis:::::She s trying to follow the rules When Elizabeth Hotchkiss stumbles upon a copy of How to Marry a Marquis in her employer s library, she s convinced someone is playing a cruel joke. With three younger siblings to support, she knows she has to marry for money, but who might have guessed how desperate she s become? A guidebook to seduction might be just the thing she needs and what harm could there be in taking a little peek? But he s making his own James Sidwell, the Marquis of Riverdale, has been summoned to rescue his aunt from a blackmailer, a task that requires him to pose as the new estate manager, and his primary suspect is his aunt s companion, Elizabeth. Intrigued by the alluring young woman with the curious little rulebook, he gallantly offers to help her find a husband by practicing her wiles on him. But when practice becomes all too perfect, James decides that there is only one rule worth following that Elizabeth marry her marquis.
