10065348:::::The Secret War:::::Jack Blank is living the dream as a celebrity superhero-in-training in the Imagine Nation. Unfortunately, Jack's dream has a nightmare lurking under the surface- a shocking connection to the enemy supersoldier Revile, and a deadly R stov plot he can't tell anyone about... not even his closest friends. When the heroes of the Imagine Nation discover that an attack by R stov sleeper agents is imminent, Jack is forced to trust his friends in a race against the clock to fight back mass hysteria, find the enemy spies, and thwart the R stov plans in time to prevent a devastating invasion.
