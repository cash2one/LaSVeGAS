21915501:::::His First Lady:::::On the surface, Evan appears to have it all. She's young and beautiful and holds one of the highest positions in the church; that of the First Lady. But her smiles are just an illusion because hidden beneath the surface, Evan struggles with making it all look good for her husband Leon; the powerful pastor of their mega church. But going up against Leon's thirst for greed and power can have deadly consequences. As his kingdom rises, Evan fights her attraction to a forbidden love while trying to hold on to everything she holds sacred as she's being led down a path of discovering the truth and finding her voice.
