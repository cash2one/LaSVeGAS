48841:::::Images from the Holocaust: A Literature Anthology:::::Images from the Holocaust is an anthology of nonfiction, poetry, fiction, and drama that explores and exhumes the Holocaust experience of the victims, the survivors, and those who had the courage to defy the horror. This comprehensive anthology examines the background of hatred that made the Holocaust possible, the day-to-day terror experienced by those who were its targets, and the painful aftermath for survivors and their descendants.
