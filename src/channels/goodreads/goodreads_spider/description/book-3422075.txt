3422075:::::Men of the Otherworld:::::As a curious six-year-old, Clayton didn t resist the bite he asked for it. But surviving as a lone child-werewolf was more than he could manage until Jeremy came along and taught him how to straddle the human-werewolf worlds, gave him a home and introduced him to the Pack. So begins this volume, featuring three of the members of the American Pack a hierarchical founding family where bloodlines mean everything and each day presents a new, thrilling, and often deadly challenge.
