614514:::::Project Paris:::::Au revoir, NYC; hello Paris! That's right -- Imogene and Evie are headed to the fashion capital of the world. Armed with internships, petit fours, and beaucoup fashion sense, this summer is going to be beyond fabulous! But what happens when the high-fashion models go on strike just days after the girls arrive? Couture week is canceled, designers are fleeing Paris, and Imogene's got a case of the gloomies. That is, until the girls "discover" a mysterious, unnamed designer who just might lure fashion back to Paris....
