19209602:::::Sensuality:::::Before Danielle was old enough to get a job her father had always told her to make her own money, be her own boss, to build her own empire. However, Danielle quickly finds that life can throw some heavy punches. She is a young woman on a mission, but on her way to grandness she finds herself entwined in mayhem with backstabbing friends, murder, and another possible heartbreak that could take her over the edge. Will she give in to love one more time and surrender to the one man who could love her like no other or will she let the past keep her from acquiring the love that she longs for?
