1243240:::::Withnail and I: The Screenplay:::::Bruce Robinson, nominated for an Academy Award for his screenplay of The Killing Fields, is one of the most original and exciting film-makers in British cinema. He is perhaps best known for writing and directing Withnail and I - now a cult classic about to celebrate its tenth anniversary. To coincide with this anniversary Bruce Robinson has written a typically witty and sharp introduction to accompany the screenplay. This book is destined to become a collectors' item and a must have for all devotees of the film.
