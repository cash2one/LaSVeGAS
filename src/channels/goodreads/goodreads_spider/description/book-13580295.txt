13580295:::::In the Dark:::::What happens when you find out the big bad wolf is the good guy and the hunter is the one to watch out for? For Zoe Sullivan the choice was made the moment Marek saved her life. From her introduction to his quirky family and their secret world, to the discovery of her own origins and the final showdown with a diabolical adversary Zoe's journey to becoming a part of Marek's pack is charged with romance, humor, and action beyond her wildest dreams.
