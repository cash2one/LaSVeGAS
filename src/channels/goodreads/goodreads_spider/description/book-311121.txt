311121:::::April Lady:::::When the new Lady Nell Cardross begins to fill her days with fashion and frivolity, the Earl has to wonder whether she really did marry him for his money, as his family so helpfully suggests. And now Nell doesn't dare tell him the truth... What with the concern over his wife's heart and pocket, sorting out her brother's scrapes and trying to prevent his own half-sister from eloping, it is no wonder that the much-tried Earl almost misses the opportunity to smooth the path of true love in his marriage...
