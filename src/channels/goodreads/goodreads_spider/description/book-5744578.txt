5744578:::::Just Enough Is Plenty: A Chanukah Tale:::::An old peddler joins Malka's family for the first night of the Hanukkah celebrations--one in which there is "just enough" food for the family, but they gladly share with him. The next morning, they find the peddler has gone, leaving a a sackful of gifts--and Malka is convinced that he was Elijah.
