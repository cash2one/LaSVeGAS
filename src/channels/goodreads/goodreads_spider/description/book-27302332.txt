27302332:::::Dodger's Doorway:::::One night, Mark 'Dodger' Bishop ventures through a magical doorway that transports him to Storyworld, a strange realm inhabited by characters from fairy tales, myths, and legends. Dodger must team up with the warrior Humpty Dumpty and the alchemist Rumpelstiltskin to complete a series of adventures. Along the way, they encounter a cannibalistic witch, a mad puppeteer, ferocious bounty hunters, and much more. While Dodger conquers these various challenges, he soon begins to conquer his own fears and insecurities as well.
