22206699:::::License to Thrill:::::The wackiest road trip in history has reached its final destination... But Coke and Pepsi McDonald aren't in the clear just yet! Before they reach the finish line, the twins will be abducted by aliens, trapped with a venomous snake, pushed through a deadly turbine, and thrown into a volcano.
