5213965:::::The Call of the Wild, White Fang, and Other Stories:::::Of all Jack London's fictions none have been as popular as his dog stories. In addition to The Call of the Wild , the epic tale of a Californian dog's adventures during the Klondike gold rush, this edition includes White Fang , and five famous short stories - Batard', Moon-Face', Brown Wolf', That Spot', and To Build a Fire'.
