32665692:::::Article Three:::::Trust will get you killed and trust will keep you alive In a world where the System governs everything, Ava s a rebel one who can control other people s thoughts with her mind. As part of a resistance movement preparing for war, this is a useful skill. Levi stopped believing in the struggle for freedom when it snatched his parents from him. Now he s just trying to live a quiet life and control the voices that threaten his sanity. One night Levi s sister is arrested. To free her, he has to break old promises and get involved with people he swore he d never associate with. Ava s ordered to help him and, together, they leave on a rescue mission. She says he has to trust the rebels. But should they?
