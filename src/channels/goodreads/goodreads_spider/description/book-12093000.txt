12093000:::::The Cahokian:::::The Cahokian is a historical novel, based on the final years of the largest North American pre-contact empire. Acoto, the Chief Warlord of Cahokia, accepts a mission in the far north-east. Determined to restore the fading glory of the Empire, he leads a campaign against the northern barbarians. However, the League of the Iroquois is not an easy target. Before the day of the crucial battle is over he discovers his life is about to take a very unexpected turn.
