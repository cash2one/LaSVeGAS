411460:::::Missing Persons:::::Psychologist Alan Gregory s friend and fellow therapist Hannah Grant has died suddenly and mysteriously. The police are baffled, leaving another unsolved homicide in Boulder, Colorado. Only Alan can decipher Hannah s clues a quest that will take him to Las Vegas and lead him to question the integrity of those closest to him.But while Alan tracks a missing patient of Hannah s, the answers to both cases may be locked inside the mind of a client he has been treating for schizoid personality disorder. Running a maze of dilemmas, Alan takes a bold risk that will cost him his career or his life.
