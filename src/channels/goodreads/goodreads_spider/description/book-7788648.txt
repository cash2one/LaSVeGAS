7788648:::::Please forgive me:::::Have you ever dreamed of running away? Of just leaving the past behind you? Leonie has done just that. She s moved to another country and got a new job, a new home, a brand new life. . . But when she discovers a box of unread love letters in the bottom of her wardrobe, all ending with the words please forgive me , she finds herself drawn to the mystery behind them. Can the unravelling of another person s story help Leonie deal with her own past and her own secrets? All she knows is that she has to try. Because if the couple behind the love letters had a happy ending, then perhaps she just might too.
